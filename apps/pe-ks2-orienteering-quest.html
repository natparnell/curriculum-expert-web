🧭 Orienteering Quest

PE · KS2 · Outdoor and adventurous activity: problem solving

Teacher aim: Pupils work as a team to plan the fastest route between control points on a projected map. They visit every control, then race the clock to compare their plan against the shortest possible path. Focus on problem solving, route choice and teamwork, not running speed.

🗺️ Orienteering map

Tap or click each control flag in the order your team chooses. Start at the orange triangle, visit all controls, then check your route.
