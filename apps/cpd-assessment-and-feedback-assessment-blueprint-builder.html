The test on your desk

Your job: A trainee teacher (Mr Okafor) drafted six questions worth 30 marks. Tag each one so the department can see whether the paper is valid (covers what was taught) and well balanced (a sensible spread of demand). Drag the objective codes onto each question, then set its cognitive demand.
