Historic environment

Site Enquiry: Kenilworth Castle, 1575

In July 1575, Robert Dudley, Earl of Leicester, hosted Queen Elizabeth I here for 19 days. The buildings he raised are still readable today. Examine each highlighted feature, then move to Section 3 to reason about what the site reveals. Tap a pin (or use Tab and Enter).

Historical reasoning

Build an Inference: What did Kenilworth reveal?

Enquiry question: "What do the physical features of Kenilworth Castle reveal about the Earl of Leicester's ambitions in 1575?" Drag each piece of evidence under the claim it best supports. Strong evidence supports the claim; weak or misused evidence does not. The investigator will judge your reasoning.
