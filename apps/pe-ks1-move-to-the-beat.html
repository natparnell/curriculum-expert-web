Move to the Beat

PE · KS1 · Dance

Teacher aim: children perform simple movement patterns and move in time to a steady beat, then build and remember a four-part class dance phrase. Project this, count the children in, and dance together. (England National Curriculum, KS1 PE: dance using simple movement patterns.)

1 Watch the beat board

👋
Press play and copy the move on every beat.
80 beats a minute

2 Build your four-part dance phrase

Drag a move into each box, or tap a move then tap a box. Use four moves to fill the phrase.

Let's make a dance! Add four different ideas for the best phrase. Try not to repeat the same move twice in a row.

3 Can the class remember it?

Watch your phrase play once. Then the boxes go blank and you put the moves back in the right order from memory.

Memory challenge First build a four-move phrase in step 2, then press "Show me, then hide".
