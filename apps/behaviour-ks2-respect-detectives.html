Behaviour & Pastoral · Key Stage 2

Respect Detectives 🔎

Teacher aim: Pupils investigate classroom and online scenes for respectful versus disrespectful behaviour, build a class respect rule, and stress-test it against tricky edge cases. Covers respectful relationships, courtesy and challenging stereotypes.

Respectful relationships Courtesy & fairness Stereotypes Online behaviour

Case file 1: Examine the scenes

Read each scene like a detective. Decide: is this behaviour respectful or disrespectful? Tap your verdict and the case notes will reveal the clue you were looking for.

Cases solved: 0 of 0. Solve them all to unlock the rule builder.

Case file 2: Build a respect rule

A good rule is short, positive and works everywhere: in class, in the playground and online. Pick one option from each box to assemble your class rule. Watch it build in the banner below.

1. Who does it protect?
2. What do we do?
3. Where does it apply?
Your respect rule will appear here as you choose.

Case file 3: Test the tricky cases

Real respect is hardest in the grey areas. For each tricky case, decide whether your rule still holds, then check the detective's verdict. There is not always a single neat answer; the thinking is the point.

Case closed, Detective.
