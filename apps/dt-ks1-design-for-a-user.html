Design for a User 🛠️

Choose a person to design for, work out what they need, then build and label a product just for them.

Teacher aim: KS1 Design. Generate ideas and communicate a purposeful, functional, appealing design for a chosen user.

1Who are we designing for?

Tap a person. Think about what their day is like and what would help them.

2Build the design

Drag features onto the product. Pick the ones that suit your user. You can move or remove a feature after placing it.

Your product
📦
Keyboard: Tab to a feature, press Enter to add it. Tab to a placed feature, press Delete to remove it.

Feature box

Some features help your user. Some do not. Choose carefully.

3Talk and draw your design
