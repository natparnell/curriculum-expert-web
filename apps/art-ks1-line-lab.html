🖌️ Line Lab

Art and Design · Key Stage 1 · Drawing: line, shape and form

Teacher aim: Pupils explore the visual element of line. They make wavy, zigzag, thick and thin lines, match each line to a feeling, then use lines to draw a creature.

1 Make a line, feel a line 0 of 4 tried

Pick a line type, then drag your finger or mouse across the paper to draw it. Every line carries a feeling.

2 Match the line to the feeling

Drag each line type onto the feeling it shows best. With a keyboard: press Enter to pick a line up, move to a feeling with Tab, then press Enter to drop it.
