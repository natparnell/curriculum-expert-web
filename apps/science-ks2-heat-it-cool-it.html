🔍 What is happening?

Start by dragging the thermometer
Water is a liquid at room temperature (about 20°C). Slide the thermometer up to heat it or down to cool it, and watch what the tiny particles do.

Substance shown: water. Freezing point 0°C, boiling point 100°C.
