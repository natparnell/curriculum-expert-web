Critique Lens 🔎

Teacher aim: pupils look closely at a projected artwork and build a spoken or written critique using the Describe, Analyse, Interpret, Judge frame. They mark composition lines, point out colour, and tag the mood, then get specific feedback on the evidence they have noticed.

The artwork

Pick a tool, then draw or click on the picture. Everything here is a made-up demonstration painting.

Keyboard: press L for a composition line, C for a colour callout, then Enter to drop one in the centre. Press Delete to remove the last mark.
