How EBacc APS and the pass thresholds are calculated

EBacc point scores follow the reformed 9 to 1 scale. The EBacc APS for a pupil is the mean of the best point score achieved in each of the five pillars, divided by 5. A pupil with no entry in a pillar scores 0 for that pillar, which drags the average down. The cohort EBacc APS averages every pupil's score.
