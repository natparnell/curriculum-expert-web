Teacher notes: what the flags mean
  • Suspension rate is reported as episodes per 100 pupils, matching DfE published statistics for suspensions and permanent exclusions.
  • Disproportionality compares a group's rate against the cohort average. A relative rate above roughly 1.5 times parity is a watch flag; above 2 times is a disproportionate flag warranting a leadership review.
  • Repeat suspensions and rising lost days are leading indicators of permanent exclusion risk, so early-help reach reduces both repeats and PEX in the model.
