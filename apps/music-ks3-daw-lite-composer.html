How this links to the National Curriculum

KS3 pupils should compose and improvise across styles and genres, drawing on a range of musical structures and styles. In this app pupils:

  • layer drums, bass, chords and melody to build musical texture;
  • arrange a 4-bar structure and decide where to add or strip back layers;
  • use a chord progression as a harmonic foundation, then write a melody that fits the key;
  • control tempo and compare how style presets change the same elements.

Elements covered: pitch, duration, dynamics through density, tempo, texture, structure and timbre (different track sounds).
