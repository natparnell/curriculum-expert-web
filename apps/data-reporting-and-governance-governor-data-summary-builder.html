Governor Data Summary Builder

Teacher aim: show governors and trustees how a school turns its headline measures into a clear, RAG-rated, one-page board report, with plain-English commentary that any non-specialist can follow.

How it works. Set this term's headline figures on the left. The app rates each measure red, amber or green against the trust's agreed thresholds, then prompts you for board-ready commentary. Type your commentary and the app flags any jargon that a governor might not understand, suggesting a plainer word. The one-page report on the right rebuilds live. All figures below are fictional demonstration data for the made-up Westhaven Academy.

Green: on or above target Amber: watch, just below target Red: significant concern
