Newton's 2nd law: a = F ÷ m. A bigger driving force or a lighter car gives more acceleration. Resistive forces (air and friction, set near 600 N here) grow with speed, so the car eventually reaches a steady top speed where forces balance (Newton's 1st law). Momentum p = m v.
