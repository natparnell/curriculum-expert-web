Risk and Reality

Weigh the short and long term effects of everyday risks, read the risk meter, then rehearse what you would actually say to stay safe.

Teacher aim: Pupils evaluate the real risks of alcohol, smoking, vaping and energy drinks, separate facts from myths, and practise confident refusal and exit strategies. Maps to KS3 physical health: drugs, alcohol, tobacco, vaping and lifestyle.
