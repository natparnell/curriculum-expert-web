Teacher aim: read pitch and rhythm from the treble stave. Pupils name notes or play the matching key, then climb through wider note ranges. Covers reading and using staff and other musical notations (KS2).

How to use it: project this to the whiteboard for a starter, or set pupils on tablets. "Name the note" asks for the letter; "Play the key" links the symbol to the keyboard; "Rhythm read-off" names note values and counts beats. A streak of five climbs a level and widens the note range.
