🎵 Draw Your Music

Teacher aim: symbols and pictures can stand for sounds (a graphic score)

Sound pen
⬆ High sounds ⬇ Low sounds Time ➡
🖍 Pick a sound pen, then draw a line or shape across the page. Press Play my music to hear what your drawing sounds like.

What your marks mean

Every mark on the page is a sound. Where you draw and how you draw it changes what we hear.

  • A line going up means the sound goes higher.
  • A long line means a long note. Hold it.
  • Small dots are short notes. Quick taps.
Listening challenge: Can you draw a sound that climbs up like a rocket, then drops back down low? Press Play to check.

For drawing with the keyboard

No mouse or finger? You can still compose. Use these buttons to add marks with the chosen sound pen, then press Play.

Tip for the whiteboard: drag slowly to draw long, smooth shapes; tap lots of dots for busy, bouncy music.
