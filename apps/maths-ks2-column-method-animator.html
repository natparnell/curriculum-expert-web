Teacher aim: step through formal written methods one digit at a time. Pupils predict each carry, exchange and digit, then get instant feedback. Covers column addition and subtraction, long multiplication, short division, order of operations (BIDMAS) and factors, multiples and primes.

Use whole numbers. Largest values are kept sensible for KS2.
