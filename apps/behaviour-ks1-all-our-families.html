👪 All Our Families

Teacher aim: Pupils explore that families come in many shapes, and recognise that all loving families share the same important things: love, care and keeping us safe. (KS1 Relationships: families and people who care for me.)

Step 1: Meet our families. Step 2: Sort what families share.

🖼️ Our family gallery

Tap a family to hear who is in it. Every one of these families is different, and every one is full of love.

Tap or select a family card to learn about it.

📊 Our class chart: what do families share?

Drag each card to the right column. All families share the warm things on the left. The things on the right just look different from family to family. Both columns are special, but only the left side is the same in every family.

Cards to sort

On a keyboard: press Enter or Space on a card to pick it up, then press 1 for "All families share this" or 2 for "Looks different in each family".

The class chart
