🍲 Sound Soup

Teacher aim: pupils experiment with, create and combine sounds to make a simple composition, then listen back to explore texture (how many sounds play together) and layering.

🎵 Pick your sounds

Drag a sound into the pot. On a keyboard, press a sound then press Enter, or use the Add buttons.

Tip: every sound is different. A drum is short and low; a bell rings high; rain is busy and soft.
