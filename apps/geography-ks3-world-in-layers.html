Teacher notes and curriculum link

Strand: KS3 Locational knowledge, extending pupils' world map to globally significant places and the world's oceans and key marine features (terrestrial and marine). The map is a deliberately simplified, stylised diagram for pattern spotting, not a survey-accurate atlas.

How to use: project the map, work through the layers as a class, then set pupils the pattern challenges. The "Show me on the map" button models where to look. Names and figures are illustrative.
