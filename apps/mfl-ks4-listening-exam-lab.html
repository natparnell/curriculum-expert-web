Examiner technique

  • Read the questions first. Predict the vocabulary you will hear before you press Play.
  • Main point vs detail. The first listen catches the gist; the second listen confirms numbers, times and reasons.
  • Opinions need reasons. Listen for parce que / porque and adjectives that carry the speaker's view.
  • Watch the time frame. A verb in the past or future can flip the correct answer.
  • Negatives reverse meaning. ne…pas / ne…jamais and no / nunca often hide the right detail.
  • Distractors. A number or word that is said but is not the answer. Match the whole question, not one word.
