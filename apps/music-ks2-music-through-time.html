Music Through Time

Teaching aim: pupils explore the history of music from the Baroque period to today, listen to a defining clip for each era, meet a great composer or musician, then test their listening by matching unknown clips to the right period.

Music Key Stage 2 History of music and great composers Listening and appraising

The timeline of music

Tap an era on the timeline to hear a clip that captures its sound and to meet a famous composer or musician from that time. Each clip is played live by the computer, so make sure the volume is up.

Choose an era above to begin. The earliest period (Baroque) is on the left and the present day is on the right.

Whose sound is this?

Listen to a mystery clip, then decide which era it belongs to. You can replay each clip as many times as you like.
