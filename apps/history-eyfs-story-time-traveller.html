🏰 Story Time Traveller

Teacher aim: explore the past through a story. Children sort story clues into long ago or today and hear why each one belongs.

Once upon a time, brave Princess Marigold lived in a tall stone castle. At night she lit her room with flickering candles. Knights rode their horses to the gate and carried shining swords. Can you find the clues that show this story happened long ago?
