Ideology Compass

Teacher aim: students locate real policy choices on a two-axis political compass (state vs market, liberty vs authority), then test how the liberal, conservative and socialist traditions each respond, sharpening understanding of ideology, sovereignty and the distribution of power.

1 Place a policy on the compass

Pick a policy, then drag its marker. Horizontal axis = how much the economy is steered by the state or the market. Vertical axis = how much power rests with the individual or with collective authority.
