How does a sculptor work? (Grown-up & child guide)

A sculpture is art you can walk around. It is not flat like a painting. It has form (a 3D shape you could hold), shape, texture (how the surface feels) and pattern (marks that repeat).

  • Add & stack: pick a shape, tap to drop it, then drop more on top to join them, like rolling and joining balls of clay.
  • Resize: tap a shape to pick it up, then use the Size slider to grow or shrink it.
  • Move: drag a shape with your finger or mouse, or use the arrow keys.
  • Texture & pattern: make the surface smooth, bumpy, scratched or dotty, just like pressing into clay.
  • Talk about it: What is your sculpture? Which artist's idea does it remind you of? What is the same and what is different?
