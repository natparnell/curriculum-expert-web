    How reading and matching helps

    Good reading in a new language is not just guessing. You look at how a word is spelled, sound it out, and link it to a meaning you already know. This game gives you that practice.

    1. Read first. Say the Spanish word quietly in your head before you match it.
    2. Look for clues. Many Spanish words look a little like English (for example "rojo" and "rosa").
    3. Use the dotted words. Hover or tap a glossary word for its meaning.
    4. Try the harder mode. Match a whole sentence like "el gato es negro" to the picture it describes.
