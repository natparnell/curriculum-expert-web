Identity Mosaic

Religion in contemporary Britain is not one thing. People layer faith, heritage, culture and practice in their own way. Build a person's identity tile by tile and see how no two believers are quite alike.

Teacher aim
Pupils explore lived religion, multiple identities and the idea of "believing without belonging" in modern Britain, recognising identity as complex, personal and changing.
