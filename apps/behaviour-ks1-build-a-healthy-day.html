Build a Healthy Day ☀️

Teacher aim: KS1 pupils plan a balanced healthy day by placing daily activities on a 24-hour wheel, covering hygiene, sleep, healthy eating and physical activity. The wheel turns green when the day is balanced.

My day wheel Drop an activity onto a time of day, or tap a slot to fill it.

Clean & tidy0
Healthy food0
Active play0
Good sleep0

A balanced day needs at least: 2 clean & tidy, 2 healthy food, 2 active play and 2 good sleep.

Activity cards Drag a card to the wheel, or use the keyboard (see below).

Let's plan a healthy day! Pick activities and place them around the wheel. Try to fill every time of day.

Keyboard: Tab to a card and press Enter to pick it up, then Tab to a wheel slot and press Enter to drop it. Press Delete on a filled slot to clear it.
