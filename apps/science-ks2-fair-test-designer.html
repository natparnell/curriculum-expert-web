🧪 Fair Test Designer

Working scientifically at KS2: plan a fair test, choose your variables, then plot the results.

Teacher aim: Pupils plan a fair test by choosing one variable to change, one thing to measure, and the things to keep the same. They then read the auto-plotted bar chart, complete a data table, and write a conclusion from the evidence.

  1. 1 Pick a question
  2. 2 Choose what to change
  3. 3 Choose what to measure
  4. 4 Keep the rest the same
  5. 5 Run it and conclude

Step 1: Pick an investigation question

Choose a question your class will investigate. Each one comes with a set of factors to sort.

Steps 2 to 4: Sort the factors

Decide what each factor should be in your fair test.

Change (the one variable you change) Measure (what you record) Keep the same (control variables)
