Citizenship · KS4 · The media & the critical citizen

Spot the Spin

Teacher aim: Pupils compare how three fictional outlets report the same event, tag examples of bias, framing and missing context, then build and justify a reliability score. Covers the role of a free press, misinformation and evaluating sources as a critical citizen.

The event everyone is reporting

All three outlets below cover the exact same council decision. The facts are identical. The reporting is not.

Agreed facts (the public record)

  • Millford Town Council voted 7 to 4 to close the High Street to cars on Saturdays for a six month trial.
  • The trial starts in September and will be reviewed by an independent panel afterwards.
  • Local shop owners are split: a survey of 40 shops found 22 in favour, 14 against, 4 undecided.
  • The change was proposed after three pedestrian injuries on the High Street last year.
