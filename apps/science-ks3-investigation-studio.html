Investigation Studio

KS3 Science · Working scientifically: plan, measure, plot, evaluate

Teacher aim: Pupils plan a fair test, choose suitable apparatus, collect repeat readings affected by random error, then plot a graph and judge the reliability of their method.

Step 1 · Plan a fair test

Read the question, then choose your variables. A fair test changes one thing and keeps everything else the same.

The investigation: Priya is testing how the height a ball is dropped from affects how high it bounces back. She drops the same rubber ball onto the same hard floor.

Step 2 · Choose your apparatus

You are measuring drop height and bounce height in centimetres. Select the apparatus you actually need. Tap to toggle each item.

Step 3 · Collect simulated data

Choose a drop height, then take a reading. Real measurements wobble because of random error, so take three repeats at each height and the app finds the mean. Watch for any anomalous result.
