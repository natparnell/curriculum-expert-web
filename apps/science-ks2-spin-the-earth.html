KS2 Science · Earth and Space

Spin the Earth 🌍

Teacher aim: Pupils explain that the Earth spins on its axis to make day and night, orbits the Sun to make a year, and that the Moon changes shape over a month as we see different amounts of its sunlit half.

Spin the Earth, move the watcher

Drag the Earth to spin it, or press Play to let it turn. Drag the orange watcher around the globe and see whether it is in daytime or night.
