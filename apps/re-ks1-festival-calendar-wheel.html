Festival Calendar Wheel 🎡

Teacher aim: Spin the year wheel to a festival, then match its story, food and symbol. Pupils explore special times in Christianity, Islam, Judaism and Hinduism (KS1 RE: festivals and special times).

🔄 The Year Wheel

Tap a festival, or press Spin. You can also use the wheel with your keyboard.

Completed:
Keyboard: Use ← → on the wheel to choose a festival, Enter to open it. In the matching task, press Enter on a card to pick it up, move to a box and press Enter to drop it.
