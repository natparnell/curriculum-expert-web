🧩 Curriculum Sequencer

Teacher aim: order the concepts in a scheme of learning so each idea is built on secure prior knowledge and points forward to where it is next used. Spot threshold concepts that unlock the rest of the unit, and check every link is sound before you commit the sequence to your medium-term plan.

CPD & Staff Tools Planning and Curriculum Sequencing & schemes of learning

Sequence builder

Drag a concept card up or down, or focus a card and use the up and down buttons (or Alt + ↑/↓) to reorder. Tap a card to read its prior links, future links and any threshold note. The dashed rails between cards show whether each step follows from the one before.

    Coherence check

    Build a sequence, then press Check this sequence. You will get specific feedback on each link, the placement of threshold concepts, and an overall coherence score.
