How it works

  • The caterpillar height follows the pitch your class sings: low notes near the soil, high notes near the top leaf.
  • Match the note builds tuneful singing by asking pupils to copy a sung pitch and hold it steady.
  • Call and response grows phrase length and vocal memory using simple do-mi-sol shapes.
  • Best in a quiet space with one clear singer at a time. Encourage a relaxed open "aah" or "oo" sound.
