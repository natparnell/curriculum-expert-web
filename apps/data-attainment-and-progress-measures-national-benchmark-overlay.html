📘 How to read this

Why a confidence interval? A single year's headline figure carries statistical uncertainty, especially in smaller cohorts. The benchmark band shows the range within which a difference is not statistically meaningful. A school sitting inside a band is performing in line with that comparator, even if the central number differs slightly.

National is every state-funded mainstream school in England. Local authority is all schools in the same LA (here, Devon). Statistical neighbours are schools with a similar intake (prior attainment, disadvantage, size), which is usually the fairest single comparison.
