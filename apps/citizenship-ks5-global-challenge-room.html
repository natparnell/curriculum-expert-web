Citizenship · Key Stage 5 · Global citizenship and contemporary world issues

Global Challenge Room

Teacher aim: Pupils negotiate a real global problem as competing nations, discovering how national interest, capacity and cooperation reshape the outcome live. Each move trades off domestic cost against the shared planetary result.

Run as a class: one device per team, or one nation per group around the board.

The Summit: emergency talks on global heating

Four nations meet to agree a 2035 emissions and climate-finance deal. The world needs deep, fairly shared cuts to stay near 1.5°C. Every nation wants action, but each also protects jobs, growth, energy security and budgets at home. Set each nation's pledge, then convene the summit to see the combined result.
