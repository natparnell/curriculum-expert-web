Choreo Canvas 💃

Teacher aim: pupils compose a short dance by arranging motifs in a sequence, choosing levels and formations, then preview the routine with the dancers and use the feedback to refine more complex patterns.

1. Your movement bank

These are your motifs (short movement ideas). Drag one onto the timeline, or tap to select it then press Add to timeline.

Tip: a good dance reuses a motif but changes it (different level, direction or formation) so it grows without becoming a list of random moves.
