Religious Education · Key Stage 3

One Religion, Many Branches

Teaching aim: a single tradition is not one fixed thing. Expand each branch to see where Christians and Muslims agree, where they differ, and (most importantly) why those branches formed. This is "lived religion": diversity within a tradition, not just between traditions.

1. Choose a tradition

Tap a tradition, then open its branches below.

2. Expand the branches

Each branch grew from a real moment in history. Open one to compare its beliefs, practices and sources of authority.
