Behaviour & Pastoral · KS5 · Mental health, independence and life transitions

Transition Resilience Lab

You are about to live the first few weeks of independent life: managing money, fighting isolation and handling a workload with no one chasing you. Build a coping and help-seeking plan, then stress-test it against what really happens.

Teacher aim: students design and pressure-test a balanced plan that combines self-regulation, planning and (crucially) knowing where adults and services actually get help. Run live on a board, in pairs or solo.

Step 1 Pick a transition to simulate

Each fictional student is starting something new. Choose one to step into. You can come back and try the others.
