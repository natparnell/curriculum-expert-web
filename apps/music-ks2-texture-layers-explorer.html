Music · KS2 · Listening & Appraising

Texture Layers Explorer

Teacher aim: pupils strip out and add back instrumental layers of a track, then use the words thin and thick (texture), along with tempo, dynamics and timbre, to describe what they hear.

1The mixing desk

Tap each layer to switch it on or off. Listen: fewer layers sound thin, more layers sound thick. Then check the texture meter.

Press Play to start the track.

Right now the texture is silent

Keyboard: use Tab to reach a layer, then Space or Enter to switch it on or off.

2Form mapper

Songs are built from sections. Tap a section to jump the band there. Notice how the texture changes: the chorus is thicker than the verse.

3Listening challenge

Set the layers as the question asks, listen carefully, then choose the best answer. The app tells you exactly why.
