Iterative Design Loop

KS4 Design and Technology · Designing and making principles

Teacher aim: Pupils run a full GCSE design cycle (explore, create, evaluate, refine) on one product, using user feedback, ergonomics and tolerances to drive measurable improvement across versions.

1. Explore the brief

Before drawing anything, designers analyse who the product is for and what matters most. Set your design priorities. You have a limited "focus budget", so you cannot make everything equally important.

Brief: Design a handheld reusable water bottle for Year 7 pupils to carry between lessons. It must fit in a side pocket of a school bag, be easy to open one-handed, and survive being dropped. Three test users are waiting to try your prototype.
