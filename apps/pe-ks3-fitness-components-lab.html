Fitness Components Lab

KS3 Physical Education · Fitness, health and active lifestyles

Teacher aim Pupils identify the components of fitness, link them to sports, build a balanced training circuit and model how exercise intensity changes heart rate.

Match each sport to the fitness component it needs most

Drag a sport onto the component it relies on most heavily. On a tablet or whiteboard, drag with your finger. Using a keyboard? Press Enter on a sport to pick it up, then press Enter on a component to drop it.
