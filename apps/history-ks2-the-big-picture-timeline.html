The Big Picture Timeline

Teaching aim: KS2 chronological understanding. Place studied periods on one proportionally scaled BC/AD ribbon, then read off which civilisations overlapped and how long ago each one was.

1. Build the ribbon

Drag each period onto the ribbon. The bar snaps to the years it really happened, scaled to size. A longer bar means a longer period; bars further left happened longer ago.

What pupils should notice

  • Ancient Egypt began long before the Romans, and lasted a very long time.
  • The Romans in Britain and the early Vikings nearly touch, but the Romans came first.
  • Even the Vikings were over a thousand years ago: history is much older than it feels.
