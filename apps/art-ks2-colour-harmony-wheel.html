Colour Harmony Wheel

Teaching aim: pupils explore how colours relate on the wheel (complementary, analogous, warm and cool) and see each colour scheme come to life on a sample painting. Builds visual-elements vocabulary: hue, harmony, contrast, warm, cool.

🎨 The colour wheel

Drag the white dot, or use the buttons below to choose a scheme. Watch the matching colours light up.

Keyboard: focus the wheel, then press ← / → to move the chosen colour. Use the scheme buttons with Tab and Enter.

The colours below come straight from the wheel. Each scheme behaves differently.
