Here and There Compare

Teacher aim: compare a small UK village with a hot place far away (Place knowledge, KS1).

Look closely at both places. One is Brindlecombe, a little village in England. The other is Lemon Bay, a hot seaside town far away in Africa. Drag each feature card into the right part of the sorting boxes: only in the UK village, in both places, or only in the hot place.

🏴󠁧󠁢󠁥󠁮󠁧󠁿 Brindlecombe, England (UK)
Cool and rainy. Stone houses, green fields, an oak tree and a tarmac road.
🏝️ Lemon Bay, a hot place far away
Hot and sunny. Wooden huts with straw roofs, sandy ground, palm trees and a dusty road.
Sort every feature into the boxes below. Sorted: 0 of 8

Feature cards (drag one into a box, or tap to pick it up):

🏠 Only in the UK village

Things you find in Brindlecombe but not in the hot place.

🔗 In both places

Things that are the same in both places.

🌴 Only in the hot place

Things you find in Lemon Bay but not in the UK village.

Pick a card to begin. Think: is this thing in the UK village, the hot place, or both?

🌟 Brilliant comparing!

You sorted every feature correctly.

Keyboard: press Tab to a card, then press Enter or Space to pick it up and choose a box.
