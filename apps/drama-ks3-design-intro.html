Reflect like a designer

  1. Which single design choice did the most to create the mood? Why?
  2. What would change for the audience if you turned the brightness right down?
  3. Could two of your choices ever contradict each other and confuse the audience?
  4. Pick a different scene. Which choices had to change, and which stayed the same?
