Watch and Critique

KS3 Drama · Evaluating and responding to live and recorded theatre

Teacher aim: Pupils watch a short performance, pause at marked moments, and write evidence-based critique about acting, design and impact. Responses collate into one shared class review you can project and discuss.

1. Watch the clip

Press play. The clip pauses itself at four marked moments (gold dots). At each pause a critique prompt appears on the right.
