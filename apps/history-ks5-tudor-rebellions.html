Evaluating a source on rebellion

Paper 3 Section A asks you to assess the value of a source for an enquiry. Choose a source, note its nature, origin and purpose (NOP), then reveal a model evaluation. Always weigh value and limitation, and use your own knowledge to test the source.

Enquiry

Nature

What type of source is it (letter, proclamation, chronicle, depositions)? What does the form imply?

Origin

Who produced it, when, and how close were they to events? Were they a participant, official or observer?

Purpose

Why was it created, and for whom? What did the author want the audience to think or do?
