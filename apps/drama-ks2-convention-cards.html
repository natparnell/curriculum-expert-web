Drama · Key Stage 2

Convention Cards

Teacher aim: pupils learn four named drama conventions (thought-tracking, narration, mime and conscience alley), watch a worked example, then apply each one to a shared class stimulus on screen.

NC strand: drama techniques and conventions
1 Flip a card 2 Watch the example 3 Apply it to our stimulus

🃏 The convention deck

Tap or press a card to flip it. Each card shows the technique with a tiny animated example. Press the replay strip to play it again.

🎭 On the whiteboard: flip one card, watch the example together, then ask "who could perform this for us?" before moving to the apply task below.

📌 Apply it to our stimulus

Use the same scene for every convention. Read the stimulus, then choose the line that uses each technique correctly. You get instant feedback that tells you why.

🚪
Our shared stimulus:

A child called Priya stands outside a new school on her very first morning. The door is shut. She is holding her bag tightly.
