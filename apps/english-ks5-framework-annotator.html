English Language · A-level · KS5

Framework Annotator

Teaching aim: Model systematic analysis of language data. Pupils tag features by framework (lexis, grammar, phonology, pragmatics, discourse), justify the effect, and track language change and variation over time.

The data

Choose a text, then click a word or phrase to tag it. Pick the framework, then write an effect note. The app checks your label against analyst tags and scores your coverage. Demonstration data uses fictional speakers.

Tip: click a word, or use Tab to move through words and press Enter to select. Selected words highlight; choose a framework below.
