🎭 Action Song Stage

Teacher aim: children sing a nursery rhyme and perform the matching action for each line as it lights up, building a whole-class performance. Strand: singing, rhyme and musical performance.

Incy Wincy Spider

0 of 4 lines
👋 Press Play next line or tap any line to start the performance.

How to lead it: project on the whiteboard, sing the lit line together and copy the bobbing action icon. Tap a line to revisit it, or press Perform whole song to run the full performance hands-free. Each finished line earns a star towards a full curtain call.
