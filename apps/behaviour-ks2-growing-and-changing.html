Growing and Changing

KS2 Behaviour & Pastoral · Health Education · The changing adolescent body and puberty

Teacher aim: Pupils explore an age-appropriate timeline of the physical and emotional changes of puberty, separate facts from myths, and match each change to a sensible self-care habit. Calm, factual, and reassuring.

The changes timeline

Puberty happens at different times for everyone, often between about 8 and 14 years old. Slide along the ages to see which changes usually begin. There is no right or wrong age to start.
