World Music Atlas 🌍

Teacher aim: Pupils explore how musical traditions developed across cultures and continents. They listen to gamelan, samba, raga and West African drumming, identify the features that define each, then prove their listening in a sound-quiz. Covers the KS3 strand "music history, traditions and world musics".

1. Tap a place to listen

Each marker plays a short, synthesised flavour of that tradition. Use the markers, the buttons, or your keyboard (Tab then Enter).

2. What makes it special

Select a tradition on the map to see its defining features.
