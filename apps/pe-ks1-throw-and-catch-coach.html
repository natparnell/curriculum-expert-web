Throw and Catch Coach

Teacher aim: model and assess KS1 fundamental movement skills (run, jump, throw, catch). Pause the slow-motion action to break down the technique, then capture the class self-assessment thumbs.

Slow-motion technique breakdown

Choose a skill, then play the slow-motion action. Tap Pause (or use the slider) on any step to read the coaching cue and the thing to look for.

Class self-assessment: thumbs check

After practising the chosen skill, tap a thumb for each pupil. Thumbs up: I can do it well. Thumbs sideways: getting there. Thumbs down: I need more help. The class summary updates live.
