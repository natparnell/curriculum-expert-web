Citizenship · KS4 · Active citizenship

Action Project Planner 📣

Teacher aim: Students plan, run and evaluate a citizenship social action project, choosing an issue, an advocacy method and a measurable impact, while predicting and responding to how stakeholders react to their tactics.

1 Choose the issue you will act on

A social action project starts with a real concern in your community. Pick one issue. Your choice shapes who has power over the outcome.

2 Write a SMART aim

An effective campaign has a clear, measurable target, not just a feeling. As you type, the app checks whether your aim is Specific, Measurable and Time-bound.

Specific Measurable Time-bound Start typing your aim.

3 Build your advocacy method

Choose the tactics for your campaign. Each one is legal, but each has trade-offs in reach, public support and disruption. Watch the stakeholder board on the right respond as you select.
