Diet Profiler

GCSE Design and Technology: nutrition, diet-related health and food provenance. Build a day of meals for a chosen person, analyse energy and nutrient balance, then adjust to meet their needs.

Teacher aim: Pupils apply knowledge of macronutrients, energy requirements and dietary needs of different groups to plan, evaluate and modify a balanced day of meals, linking food choice to health and provenance.
