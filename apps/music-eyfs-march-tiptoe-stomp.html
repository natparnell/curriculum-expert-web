For the teacher
  • Pulse is the steady beat that stays the same; tempo is how fast or slow that beat goes. This app lets children hear and move to three tempos.
  • Tiptoe is slowest and lightest, March is a clear walking pulse, Stomp is slow and heavy. Encourage whole-body copying: knees, arms, voices.
  • The drum game scores how evenly children tap on the beat. Green stars mean a steady pulse; amber means keep listening and try to match the glow.
  • No real children are represented. All names and data are made up for demonstration.
  • Works offline on tablets and interactive whiteboards. The spacebar or enter key also taps the drum for keyboard users.
