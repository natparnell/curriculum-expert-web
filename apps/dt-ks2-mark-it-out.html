How to be accurate (tips for makers)
  • Always measure from the zero line of the ruler, not from the very end of the strip.
  • Read the scale with your eye straight above the mark to avoid a reading error.
  • A tolerance is how far out you are allowed to be. Stay inside it and the part will fit.
  • Use the try-square to check the cut line is square (at a right angle) to the edge.
  • Keyboard: focus the red marker, then use Left and Right arrows for 1 mm, hold Shift for 0.5 mm.
