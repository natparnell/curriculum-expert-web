Choose Your Tools 🛠️

Pick the right tools and materials, then make in the right order.

Teacher aim: Pupils select suitable tools and materials for a making job, then sequence the make as cut, shape, join and finish.
Design and Technology Key Stage 1 Make: tools, materials and finishing

1 Choose your making job

Tap a job. Mira and Jamal need help making something for the class.

2 Choose your tools and materials

Drag the things you need into the basket. Leave out the ones that are wrong for this job.

🧰 Tool box

📦 Materials shelf

👜 My basket (empty)

Drag items here, or use the keyboard.

Keyboard: press Enter or Space on an item to move it to or from the basket.

3 Put the making steps in order

A good maker works in this order: cut, then shape, then join, then finish. Use the arrows to sort the steps.

    Keyboard: tap the up and down arrow buttons to move a step. ↑ moves it earlier, ↓ moves it later.

    4 Watch it being made

    Press the button to make your item one step at a time.
