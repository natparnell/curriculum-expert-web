Smart Material Showcase

KS4 Design and Technology · Core technical principles

Teacher aim: explore how modern and smart materials respond to a stimulus (heat, force or electric current), read the property change as a working system, then match each material to a real product. Covers materials and their properties, smart and modern materials, energy and systems.
