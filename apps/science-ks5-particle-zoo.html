Particle Zoo ⚛

Teacher aim: students build hadrons from up, down and strange quarks, check that charge and baryon number are conserved, then read Feynman-style diagrams of fundamental interactions. Covers the standard model strand of A-level nuclear and particle physics.

Build a hadron from quarks

Drag quarks into the build box, or focus one and press Enter to add it. Three quarks make a baryon; one quark and one antiquark make a meson. The app checks charge (Q) and baryon number (B) and names what you have made.

Conservation lab

In any particle interaction, charge and baryon number are conserved (the totals before equal the totals after). Decide whether each proposed interaction can happen. The app tells you exactly which quantity fails.
