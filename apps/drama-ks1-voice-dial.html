Teacher notes (tap to open)
  • Curriculum link: KS1 spoken language, speaking audibly and with expression; choosing volume, pace and tone to suit meaning.
  • Use it on the whiteboard with the whole class, or in pairs on a tablet.
  • The robot models a setting; children then copy it with their own voices, then listen back to themselves and self-assess.
  • Ask: did loud suit the meaning here? When does a soft voice work better? Why might sad need a slower speed?
