Our online safety rules

    How to use this with the class: Read each message aloud on the whiteboard. Ask pupils to talk to a partner, then vote with thumbs up (tap) or hands flat (stop and tell). Press the choice the class agrees on and read the feedback together. All names are pretend. The golden rule: if a message ever makes us feel unsure, worried or sad, we always stop and tell a trusted adult.
