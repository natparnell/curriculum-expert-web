What do these word parts mean?
super‑ above, beyond or extra (superhero, superstar)
anti‑ against or opposite (antifreeze, anticlockwise)
auto‑ self, by itself (autograph, automatic)
‑ate turns a word into a verb, an action you do (activate)
‑ify to make or become (simplify, magnify)

A prefix goes at the front of a root. A suffix goes at the end. Both change the meaning, and a suffix often changes how you say the word.
