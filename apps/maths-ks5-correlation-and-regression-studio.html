How to use this (keyboard and touch)

Mouse or touch: press and drag any orange point. The statistics update instantly.

Keyboard: press Tab until a point is focused, then move it with the arrow keys. Hold Shift with an arrow for a larger step. Press Enter or Space to select a point so you can remove it.

Try to drag points so they lie close to a straight line and watch the PMCC head towards 1. Pull one point far from the others to see how a single outlier drags the regression line.
