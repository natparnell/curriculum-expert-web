Spot the Technology 🔍

Teacher aim: Children recognise that technology is used in homes and on the high street, and talk about what each device helps people to do. Strand: technology in the everyday world (EYFS Computing).

⭐ Found 0 of 0

👆 Tap a thing that uses technology

Look carefully. Find something that needs power or has buttons, lights or a screen. Tap it to hear what it does.

Things to find in this scene

Each one turns green when you spot it.

For the grown-up at the front

  • Ask: "How do you know it uses technology?" Listen for power, buttons, lights, screens, sounds.
  • Ask: "What does it help people do?" before tapping, then check together.
  • Tip: tab to a device and press Enter or the space bar to spot it without a touchscreen.
