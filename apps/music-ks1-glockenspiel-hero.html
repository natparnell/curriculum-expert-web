For the teacher

This activity supports the KS1 programme of study: play tuned and untuned instruments musically and experiment with, create, select and combine sounds.

  • Whole-class play-along: project the colour lane, hand out real glockenspiels and let pupils follow the falling colours together.
  • Differentiation: use Slow speed and Hot Cross Buns (three notes) to start; build to Twinkle Twinkle.
  • Untuned link: the Pattern Band develops a steady pulse, ready for layering tuned and untuned parts.
  • Accessibility: bars respond to touch, mouse, and number keys 1 to 8; the Pattern Band taps with the Space bar.
