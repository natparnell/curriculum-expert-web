For the teacher: how to use this

Pupils make three choices that each change the structure: folding (stiffer shape), layering (more material), and bracing (a triangle is the most stable shape). The load tester gives instant feedback, so pupils can predict, test and improve, just like a real designer.

Talk it through: Why did the zig-zag fold hold more than the flat sheet? What happened when you added a triangle brace? Which change made the biggest difference? Encourage the words strong, stiff and stable.
