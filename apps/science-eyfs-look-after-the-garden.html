Living things need our care: plants need water, gardens need tidying, and animals need food.

For the grown-up helping

  • Strand: EYFS Understanding the World. Children learn to care for and respect living things and the natural environment.
  • How to play: tap a tool (Water, Pull weeds, or Feed birds), then tap the matching part of the garden. The app only rewards the kind, correct match, so children think about what each living thing actually needs.
  • Talk about it: "Why is the flower drooping?" "What does a bird need in winter?" "What happens to the flowers if we let the weeds take over?"
  • On tablets and whiteboards: works by tap. For keyboard, use Tab to reach a garden part and press Enter or Space to use the chosen tool.
