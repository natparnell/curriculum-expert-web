Our Day Picture Timeline

Teacher aim: Build a sense of belonging and security. Children order the pictures of our day together each morning, learn what comes next, and remember the grown-ups who keep them safe. Strand: sense of belonging, routines and being safe (EYFS).

Order Our Day

Put the pictures in the right order, from first thing in the morning to home time. Drag a card into a number box, or use the keyboard.

My Safe Grown-Ups

If you feel worried, poorly, or you need help, you can always tell a safe grown-up. Tap a grown-up to hear how they help. These are pretend people in our classroom.
