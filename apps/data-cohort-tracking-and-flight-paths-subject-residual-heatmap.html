Subject Residual Heatmap

Cohort Tracking and Flight Paths · Data & Analytics · Residuals against each pupil's own average

Teacher aim: pupils learn that a subject grade only tells half the story. By comparing each grade to the pupil's own mean, we expose where a subject is over or under performing for that child, the building block of value-added and departmental review.

The residual heatmap

Pick a pupil, then select any cell. Colour shows the residual: how far that subject sits above or below the pupil's mean grade.
