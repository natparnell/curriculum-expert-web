English · KS4 · Spoken Language Endorsement: prepared formal presentation in Standard English

Teacher aim: Pupils storyboard a 7 to 10 minute formal talk (hook, structure, close, Q&A) and use the live Standard English checker to remove informalisms before they rehearse. Build the plan, watch the readiness score respond, then export the cue cards.

1. Open with a hook

Grab the room in the first 20 seconds. Try a striking statistic, a short anecdote, a bold question or a vivid image. Keep it formal: this is assessed Standard English.

    2. Build the structure

    A clear three-part body keeps a 7 to 10 minute talk on track. Give each point a signpost (for example "My first argument is...") and one piece of evidence.
