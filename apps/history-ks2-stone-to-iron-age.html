Stone Age to Iron Age: How Life Changed in Britain

Teaching aim: to understand how life in Britain changed across the Stone Age, Bronze Age and Iron Age, from wandering hunter-gatherers to settled farmers living in metal-using communities, and to use evidence such as Skara Brae and Stonehenge to explain that change.
