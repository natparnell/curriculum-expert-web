Idea Web Studio 🕸️

Art and Design · KS2 · Developing ideas and reviewing work in sketchbooks

Teacher aim: Pupils gather ideas from a theme into a mood board, choose the strongest, then plan and self-evaluate a development sequence the class can critique together.

1 Choose your theme word

Pick a theme. The studio fills a mood board with ideas you might use. Real artists collect lots of ideas before they choose.

2 Your mood board

Each idea is a colour, shape, mark, material or feeling. Drag the ones you like best into the development sequence below. You can also press an idea and use the buttons that appear. Aim for a mix of types.

Choose a theme and press "Build mood board" to begin.

3 Plan your development sequence

Drag your strongest ideas into the four stages. This is the order an artist works in: first the spark, then trying things out, then choosing, then the final plan.

Stage 1: First idea Drop an idea here
Stage 2: Experiment Drop an idea here
Stage 3: Refine Drop an idea here
Stage 4: Final plan Drop an idea here

4 Review your work together

When you have placed some ideas, press "Critique my sequence" for feedback. Read it as a class, then improve your plan and try again.
