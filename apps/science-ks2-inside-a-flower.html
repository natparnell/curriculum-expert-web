🌼 Inside a Flower

The four stages

  1. Pollen is made in the anther (the male part).
  2. A bee carries pollen and lands on the sticky stigma. This is pollination.
  3. A pollen tube grows down to the ovary. This is fertilisation.
  4. The fertilised ovules become seeds inside the ovary.

Did you know?

Water moves up the stem through tiny tubes, a bit like a straw. Scientists call this transport. Put a white flower in coloured water and after a day the petals turn that colour. Fictional pupils Priya and Tom tried this with food colouring and saw blue petals.
