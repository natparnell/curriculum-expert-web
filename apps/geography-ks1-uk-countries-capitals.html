For grown-ups: what is this teaching?

This activity supports the Key Stage 1 National Curriculum for Geography (locational knowledge). Children learn to:

  • name and locate the four countries of the United Kingdom: England, Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland;
  • name the capital city of each country: London, Edinburgh, Cardiff and Belfast;
  • name the seas surrounding the UK: the Atlantic Ocean, the Irish Sea, the North Sea and the English Channel.

Start in Explore the Map to discover each country and sea, then use Match Capitals and Name the Seas to check what has been learned. The map is a friendly, simplified picture of the UK; it is designed to be clear for young children, not perfectly accurate in shape.
