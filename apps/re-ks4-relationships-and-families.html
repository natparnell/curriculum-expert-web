RE · GCSE · AQA 8062 · Theme A: Relationships & Families

Relationships & Families

Teaching aim: to help students compare and evaluate Christian, Hindu and non-religious responses to questions about sexuality, marriage, divorce, the family and gender equality, using accurate sources of authority, and to structure a 12-mark evaluative answer in the AQA style.

This Theme A study tool has two working stations. The Issue Comparator lays out three viewpoints side by side with their reasons and scriptural or ethical sources. The Evaluation Arena lets you build a 12-mark answer by sorting arguments for and against a statement, then reveals a model paragraph structure. All teachings below show diversity of view within and between traditions; every position is presented with respect.
