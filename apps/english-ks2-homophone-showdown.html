Punctuation reminder card
  • ’Possessive apostrophe: shows belonging. One owner, add 's (the dog's bowl). Plural already ending in s, add only ' (the dogs' park).
  • ,Comma: separates items in a list and marks a short pause (We packed apples, pears and a flask.).
  • :Colon: introduces a list or an explanation (You need three things: a pen, a ruler and hope.).
  • ;Semicolon: joins two closely linked full sentences (It rained all day; we stayed inside.).
  • —Dash: adds a sudden extra thought or a pause for effect (We opened the box and it was empty.).
