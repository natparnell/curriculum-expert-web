Two Stars and a Wish ⭐

Drama, Key Stage 2: responding to and evaluating a performance.

Teacher aim: pupils watch a peer performance, then build a kind, specific evaluation by choosing two strengths (stars) and one improvement (a wish), using drama vocabulary.

Watch the performance

Read the scene together. Imagine you are in the audience watching it on stage.

Scene: The Lost Treasure Map

Performer: Priya plays a brave explorer who has just found a hidden map.

Priya steps forward with her back straight and her chin up. She holds the map high so the whole audience can see it. Her voice is clear and loud as she says, "At last, the map is mine!" She freezes in a strong, still position. When the storm starts, her face shows worry, but her words come out a little too fast and quiet to hear at the back.

Feedback word bank

Drag a card onto the board. Gold cards are strengths (stars), blue cards are improvements (wishes).

Keyboard: press Enter or Space on a card to pick it up, then press S to send it to the Stars zone or W to send it to the Wish zone. Press Esc to put it down again.
