Care for Our World 🌍

Teaching aim: to understand that we all share one world, and that small, everyday choices about waste, energy, water, travel and nature add up to look after our local area and the wider planet. We learn how individuals and communities can act as responsible citizens and make a real difference.

1 Build a Greener Town

You look after a small town and its school. Each year you can choose one action that helps the environment. Watch how cleanliness, waste, energy use, wildlife and community happiness change over time. Good choices add up year after year.

    4 Talk About It

    Reduce, reuse, recycle. Which of these three do you think helps the planet the most, and why? Can you think of one thing your family throws away that could be reused instead?
    Getting around. How do most people travel to your school? What would make it easier for more people to walk, cycle or scoot? Who would need to help make that happen?
    Small actions, big difference. One person saving water seems tiny. What happens when a whole class, school or town does it together? Why do communities matter for looking after the environment?
