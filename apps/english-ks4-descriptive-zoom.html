Descriptive Zoom

Teacher aim: pupils build a structured descriptive piece by panning and zooming into a single image. Each zoom tier (wide shot to close detail) prompts a different sensory and crafting focus, modelling how strong description moves the reader's eye deliberately rather than listing everything at once.

🔍 The Stimulus: a harbour at dusk

Drag to pan. Use the controls, the slider, or arrow keys to zoom. The tier label updates as you move from wide shot to close detail.
