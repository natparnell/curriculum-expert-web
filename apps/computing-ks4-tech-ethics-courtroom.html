The statute book 📖

Three Acts cover almost everything you will meet at GCSE.

  • 🔐 Data Protection Act 2018 / UK GDPR
    Protects personal data. Data must be used fairly, kept secure, used only for stated purposes and not held longer than needed. People can ask to see or delete their data.
  • 💻 Computer Misuse Act 1990
    Makes unauthorised access to computers and data illegal: hacking, guessing passwords, planting malware, or going beyond the access you were given.
  • ©️ Copyright, Designs and Patents Act 1988
    Protects creative work: music, images, code, video, writing. Copying or sharing without the owner's permission infringes copyright.
  • ⚖️ No law broken (ethics / environment)
    Some actions are legal yet raise ethical or environmental concerns: fairness, bias, e-waste, energy use. The law has limits.
