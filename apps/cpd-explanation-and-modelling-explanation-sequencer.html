🧩 Explanation Sequencer

CPD & Staff Tools · Explanation and Modelling · Sequence, script and sense-check a clear explanation before you teach it

Teacher aim: A clear explanation moves in small, logical steps. Each new idea should rest on knowledge pupils already hold, with no leaps and no buried ambiguity. Order the steps below, then sense-check your sequence for prior-knowledge dependencies and unclear points.

1. Choose an explanation to sequence

Each scenario gives you the steps of a real explanation in scrambled order. Pick one to begin.

2. Build the teaching order

Drag a step from the left into the sequence on the right, or focus a step and use the Move buttons. Aim for the smallest sensible jump between each step.
