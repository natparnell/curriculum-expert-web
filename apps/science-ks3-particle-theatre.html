KS3 Science · Chemistry: Particle Model

Particle Theatre 🎭

Teacher aim: Heat and cool a substance and watch the particles change arrangement and energy across solid, liquid and gas. Switch modes to investigate gas pressure and diffusion. Pupils explain changes of state using the particle model.

Keyboard: focus the stage, then press ↑ to heat or ↓ to cool. In Pressure mode use ← / → to move the piston. In Diffusion mode press Enter to release the scent.

What is happening?

Solid state

Particles are packed in a fixed, regular pattern. They cannot move past each other, only vibrate on the spot. Press Heat to give them more energy.

A drop of scent (orange particles) is released in the corner. Watch it spread to fill the room. Heat speeds it up; cooling slows it down.
