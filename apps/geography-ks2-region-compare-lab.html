🌍 Region Compare Lab

Teacher aim: pupils compare a UK region with an overseas region using climate, land use and population, building geographical vocabulary and reasoning about similarities and differences.

Geography • KS2 place knowledge • comparative region study (UK, Europe, the Americas)

1. Choose two regions to compare

Pick one region in the United Kingdom and one region overseas, in Europe or the Americas.

2. Move the sliders to reveal the data

Each slider changes what the comparison bars show. Drag to explore climate, land use and population side by side.

🌡️Climate: month of the year July

Slide through the year to see typical temperature and rainfall in each region.

🌾Land use: focus All land use

Slide to focus on one type of land use, or show all of them together.

👥Population: zoom Whole region

Zoom from the largest city out to the whole region's population.
