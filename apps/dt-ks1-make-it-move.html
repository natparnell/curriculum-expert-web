D&T · KS1 · Technical knowledge: simple mechanisms

Make It Move 🛠️

Teaching aim: pupils explore levers, sliders and wheels and axles by moving the handle on a picture, watching the mechanism work, and naming the pivot and how far things move.

👆 Move the handle

Tip: drag the yellow handle. On a keyboard, click it then use the arrow keys.

For the teacher: project this on the whiteboard. Ask pupils to point to the pivot on the lever, then predict what happens before they move the slider. Use the word bank on the right.

💬 What is happening?

👋 Choose Lever, then drag the yellow handle. Watch the hand wave around the pivot.
How far did it move: 0

pivot the fixed point it turns on lever a bar that turns on a pivot
