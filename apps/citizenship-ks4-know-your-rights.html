Know Your Rights

Citizenship • Key Stage 4 • The legal system, justice and the rights of citizens

Teacher aim: Pupils work through real-world case trees on consumer, employment and police-encounter rights, choosing actions and discovering the legal outcome, the relevant law, and the remedy available to a citizen.

Choose a case to investigate

Each case puts you in a everyday situation where your legal rights are tested. Pick the best action at each step. The law responds. Aim for the strongest outcome.

Tip: press 1–4 to choose, or use Tab and Enter.

Demonstration resource for classroom use. Fictional characters only. Reflects the law of England and Wales for teaching purposes; not legal advice.
