Routine Designer & Rehearsal Planner

Teacher aim: design tight entry, transition and exit routines step by step, generate a rehearsal script and consistency checklist, then time your class until each routine is fast, calm and automatic. Establishing and maintaining classroom routines is the foundation of a settled room.
