Vocabulary & EAL Support Planner

CPD & Staff Tools · Adaptive Teaching · High-quality teaching for disadvantaged and EAL pupils

Teacher aim: Plan tiered vocabulary teaching and targeted language scaffolds for one lesson, so that disadvantaged and EAL pupils can access the same ambitious content as everyone else.
Sort each key word into Tier 1, 2 or 3, then attach the scaffolds that will make Tier 2 and Tier 3 words usable. The planner gives specific feedback as you go.

📚 Choose a lesson

Pick a worked example. The word pool below changes to match the lesson. All pupils are fictional and all data is illustrative.

🧩 Sort the vocabulary into tiers

Drag each word into a tier, or select a word with the keyboard and press 1, 2 or 3. Tier 1 is everyday words; Tier 2 is high-utility academic words across subjects; Tier 3 is subject-specific technical terms.

Tiered teaching plan
Tier 1 everyday
Common words most pupils know. Usually no pre-teaching needed.
Tier 2 academic
High-utility words across subjects. The biggest gap for disadvantaged pupils. Teach explicitly.
Tier 3 technical
Subject-specific terms. Often new to all pupils. Teach with a clear definition and example.
Select a word in Tier 2 or Tier 3 to plan its language scaffolds.

📊 Live feedback

The planner checks your tiering and your scaffold choices against what closes vocabulary gaps for EAL and disadvantaged learners.
