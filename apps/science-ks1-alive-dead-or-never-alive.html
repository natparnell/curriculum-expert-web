Science • KS1 • Living things and their habitats

Alive, Dead or Never Alive? 🌿

Teacher aim: Year 2 pupils sort things into alive, dead, or never been alive, using the seven life processes (MRS GREN) as clues, then match small creatures to the right microhabitat.
Stars: 0

Living things do all seven of these. We call them MRS GREN.

🚶 Movement 👶 Reproduction 👅 Sensitivity 🌱 Growth 💨 Respiration 💩 Excretion 🍴 Nutrition

Things to sort

Drag a card into a bin, or tap a card then tap a bin. Each card has its own MRS GREN clue.

💚 Alive

It does all of MRS GREN now.

🍂 Dead

It was alive, but is not any more.

🪨 Never alive

It has never, ever been alive.

Give each minibeast the right home

A microhabitat is a tiny home. Drag or tap each creature to the place it likes to live.

🪵 Under a rotting log

Dark and damp.

💧 In a pond

Cool water.

🌼 On flowers and leaves

Sunny and bright.
Welcome, scientists! 🔬 Pick up a card and decide: is it alive, dead, or has it never been alive?
