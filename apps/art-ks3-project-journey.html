What do the four objectives mean?

AO1 Develop: investigate the work of artists, cultures and contexts to inform your ideas.

AO2 Refine: experiment with media, materials, techniques and processes; review and revisit ideas.

AO3 Record: capture ideas, observations and insights from first-hand sources as the work progresses.

AO4 Present: pull it all together into a personal, meaningful response that connects to your starting point.
