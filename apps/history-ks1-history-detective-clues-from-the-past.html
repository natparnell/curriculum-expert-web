🔍 History · KS1 History Detective

History Detective: Clues from the Past

Teacher aim: Children act as history detectives. They examine three different sources (an old object, an old photograph and a line from a diary), then answer what does this tell us? Each good answer pins a clue to the class evidence wall. This builds the disciplinary idea that we find out about the past from real evidence, and that different sources tell us different things.

Our Case: The Day of the Big Seaside Trip

Long ago, a class of children went on a steam train to the seaside for the day. We were not there, so how can we know what happened? Let us look for clues. Tap each source to examine it.
