A-Level Drama · Devising original theatre in the style of a practitioner
Teacher aim: Students choose a practitioner, then make rehearsal-room choices that the tool tests against that practitioner's style, before drafting assessed working-notebook entries that explain and justify each decision.

The notebook is assessed on your justification, not just description. Answer the three reflective stems.
