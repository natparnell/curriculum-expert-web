KS1 Design & Technology • Evaluate

Good Design Detective 🔍

Teacher aim: pupils explore an existing product, judge it against simple agreed design criteria (tick or cross), then judge their own make the same way. Covers the KS1 Evaluate strand: explore existing products and evaluate their own ideas against design criteria.

Choose a product to investigate

Tap a product to put it on the big screen. We will become design detectives and decide if it is well made.

Tip for the whiteboard: pick the product first, then talk about it together as a class.

Be the detective

🧴 Product

For each clue, tap the green tick if the product does it well, or the red cross if it does not. The detective will tell you what to look for.

    Detectives explain their thinking. Ask: how do you know? Show me the part you mean.

    Judge our own make

    Now use the very same clues to judge something the class designed and made. Did our make do the job?

      Detective report: how do they compare?

      Good evaluators say what worked and one thing they would change next time.
