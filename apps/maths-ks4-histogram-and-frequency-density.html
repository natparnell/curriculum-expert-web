Histogram and Frequency Density

Teacher aim: Pupils adjust class-interval widths and frequencies and watch bars rescale by frequency density, seeing why a histogram with unequal intervals is not a naive bar chart. Fold in cumulative frequency, the median and the interquartile range.

The plot

Marks scored by a fictional Year 11 class (out of 100). Drag the white handles on top of each bar, or use the table on the right.

Bar height = frequency Bar height = frequency density

Keyboard: Tab to a handle, then ↑/↓ to change its frequency.

The data

Change a class width or a frequency. Frequency density = frequency ÷ class width.
