Step 1: Pick a local issue to act on

An active citizen spots a problem in their community and decides to make a difference. Choose one issue near our school to build a campaign about.

Step 2: Build a campaign for your issue

Drag an action onto the planning board, or press Enter on an action (or the + button) to add it. Watch the impact meter respond. A strong campaign mixes raising awareness, gathering support, and reaching decision makers.
