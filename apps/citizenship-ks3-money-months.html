Key idea: the cost of credit

Saving vs borrowing

  • Savings earn a little interest each month (your money grows slowly).
  • Borrowing charges far more interest (the debt grows quickly).
  • Interest that piles onto interest is called compound interest.
  • An emergency fund means you can pay for shocks without borrowing.
