Rule or Law?

Citizenship · Key Stage 2 · Rules, laws and why society needs them

Teaching aim: Pupils sort statements into school rule, law or neither, then explore the consequence ladder to understand why laws exist and who makes them.
Sorted: 0 of 0 Correct: 0

Step 1: Sort the statements

Drag each card into the bin you think it belongs in. The app tells you straight away if you are right, and gives you a reason.

What is the difference? A school rule is made by your school and only applies in school. A law is made by Parliament (the government) and everyone in the country must follow it. Neither means it is just a good habit or kind choice, with no official punishment.

Keyboard or whiteboard: press Tab to a card, press Enter or Space to pick it up, then Tab to a bin and press Enter to drop it.
