Stress and Resilience Studio

KS4 Behaviour & Pastoral: mental health, stress, self-care and exam wellbeing.

Teacher aim: pupils build a personalised, balanced coping toolkit against realistic exam and life stressors, then test it across a simulated pressure week and reflect on what kept their stress and energy in a healthy range.

Build your coping toolkit

Help seeking matters most. A strong toolkit is not about coping alone. Talking to a trusted adult, a tutor, the pastoral team or a service like a GP is itself a top coping strategy. If stress ever feels too big to manage, telling someone is the brave and sensible choice.
