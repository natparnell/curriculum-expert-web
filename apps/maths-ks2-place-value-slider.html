Place Value Slider

Teaching aim: read, build and round whole numbers to 10,000,000, see the effect of multiplying or dividing by 10, count across zero, and link to Roman numerals. (KS2 Number and place value.)

Build a number

Use the plus and minus buttons to set each digit. Watch the value, the words and the Roman numeral change as you go.

zero
Tap a plus button to start building.

Tip: each column is worth ten times the one to its right. Adding 1 to the tens adds ten to the total.

Round it

Drag the marker along the line, or use the arrow keys, then drop it. The line snaps to the nearest rounded value. Decide which way it should round.

Slide the marker to where you think 3742 rounds to.

Rule of thumb: look at the digit just to the right of the place you are rounding to. 5 or more rounds up; 4 or less rounds down.

Across zero

Negative numbers go below zero, like a cold temperature. Drag the thermometer marker or use the arrow keys, then answer the question.

Counting up means moving right (warmer). Counting down means moving left (colder). Crossing zero changes a minus to a plus.
