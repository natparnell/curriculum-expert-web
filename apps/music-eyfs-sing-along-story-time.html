🎵 Sing-Along Story Time

Teacher aim: children join in with and perform familiar nursery rhymes, following a bouncing ball that hops from word to word, then record the whole class singing and play it back together. Music, EYFS: singing songs, rhymes and chants.

1. Pick a rhyme and sing along

Press Sing. Watch the ball bounce on each word. Sing the highlighted word out loud.

Tip: start slow, then move the snail-to-rabbit slider to speed up once everyone knows the words.

Ready to sing.

2. Record the class singing

Press the red dot, sing together, press it again to stop. Then press Play it back so the whole class can hear.

0:00 Nothing recorded yet.

3. Pat-a-Cake Clap Map

Press Start the beat, then clap or pat the cells on your turn. Pink hands mean clap, blue rest means wait. The app tells you if your tap landed on the beat.

Tap a cell when it lights up gold.
