Big Questions Debate Builder

Pick a contemporary issue, gather religious and non-religious (Humanist) viewpoints, then build a balanced two-sided argument. The coach tells you whether your debate is genuinely balanced and well reasoned.

Teacher aim: Pupils apply religious and non-religious ethical frameworks to a current issue and construct a balanced argument that fairly represents more than one tradition. (KS3 RE: ethical reasoning.)

1 Choose your big question

Each issue has its own set of viewpoint cards from different worldviews.

2 Build a balanced argument

Drag a card into the Agree or Disagree side, or use the small buttons. On a keyboard, focus a card and press A to add to Agree, D for Disagree.
