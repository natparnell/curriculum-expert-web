🍞 Make a Jam Sandwich Robot 🤖

Teacher aim: children put the step cards in order (first, next, then, last) to write a simple set of instructions. The robot follows them exactly. Miss a step and it goes hilariously wrong, so children learn that a good algorithm is clear, ordered and complete.

📦 Step cards

Drag a card into a numbered box on the right. On a keyboard, press Enter to pick a card up, then choose a box.

📝 Our instructions

Put the cards in the right order. First at the top, last at the bottom.

Keyboard: Tab moves between cards and boxes. Enter picks up or drops a card. Esc puts a card back.
