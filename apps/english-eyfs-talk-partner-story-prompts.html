Talk Partner Story Prompts

Teacher aim: build back-and-forth talk and full spoken sentences. Project a scene, tap a glowing spot, then partners take turns to speak the sentence aloud (EYFS Communication and language: listening, attention and speaking).

How to play: choose a scene and a time word (now, before, soon). Tap a numbered glow on the picture. Partner A reads the sentence start and finishes it out loud. Partner B listens, then adds one more idea. Tap "We said it" to score the turn.
Time word:

Now: present tense (I can see). Before: past tense (I could see). Soon: future tense (I will see).
