Print Press 🖌️

Teacher aim: Pupils carve a simple stamp, ink it, then press repeats to build a pattern. They discover that printmaking makes the same mark again and again. (NC KS1: using materials creatively, including printmaking.)

1 Carve your stamp
2 Choose ink colour
3 Press repeats

Step 1: Carve the stamp Cut shapes out of the block. Cut bits print white; raised bits print colour.

Carved squares: 0 / 100 Block: 10 × 10
Keyboard: ↑↓←→ move the cursor, Enter or Space carve or re-fill that square.

Tip: carve a shape that is easy to spot, like a heart, a star or your initial. Carve at least 6 squares before you ink.

Steps 2 and 3: Ink and press Pick a colour, then tap the paper to stamp a repeat.
