Our Diverse Community 🏘️

Teacher aim: Pupils build a town from people of many backgrounds, faiths and jobs, then discover through short stories how diversity makes a community stronger.

1 Meet the people of Brookvale

Drag each person to the place where they help the community. Tap a person then tap a place if you prefer. Use the keyboard with Tab, Space and arrow keys.

Keyboard: press Space on a person to pick them up, then use Left/Right arrows to choose a place and Space again to place them. Press Escape to cancel.

2 The town of Brookvale

Place everyone to fill the town. Each person brings their own story.

The more kinds of people who join in, the stronger and kinder the community becomes. Watch the bars grow.
