      Why this matters

      In our community, everyone belongs. We all look a little different, like different things, and celebrate in different ways. Those differences make our class and our world more interesting and more colourful.

      We also share lots of things: we all want friends, we all have feelings, and we all want to be treated with kindness and respect. When we welcome someone new and make sure nobody is left out, everyone feels happier and safer.

      For grown-ups: This activity supports the KS1 Citizenship and PSHE framework strands on diversity and respect: that people are all different and all valuable; recognising similarities and differences between people (appearance, likes, celebrations, families, beliefs, and abilities); treating everyone with respect and kindness; recognising and challenging unkindness; including others; and a sense of belonging to a community. All characters are friendly, illustrative and respectful. Use the "talk about it" prompts to extend discussion.
