How does this help with GCSE performance?
The GCSE performing component is assessed on accuracy and fluency, technical control, and interpretation or expression. Practising under recording conditions, to a steady pulse, builds the control needed for the controlled assessment. Ensemble takes add a synchronisation focus, lining your part up with the rest of the group.

2. Record to the click Solo

Start the click. After a one bar count in, tap the large pad on every beat as if you were playing. The app measures how tightly your taps line up with the pulse, the engine behind accuracy and fluency.
