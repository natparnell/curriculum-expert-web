Teaching aim: Pupils plan an attack on a netball or football pitch by dragging players into space, then press Run the play to animate it and find out whether the attack works. Learning focus: attacking, defending, and finding the space.

3 Coaching points

  • Find the space: move into a gap where no defender can reach you in time.
  • Pass forward: aim to move the ball towards the goal or shooting circle.
  • Support the ball: stay close enough to receive, but not crowded together.
  • Avoid the defender: a pass that goes near a red player can be intercepted.
