Modern Foreign Languages • KS2 • French Reading

Read and Match

Teacher aim: Pupils read short illustrated French sentences and match each one to the correct picture. They tap any underlined word to look it up in a mini bilingual dictionary, practising reading for meaning and using a dictionary in context. National Curriculum (MFL KS2): read carefully and show understanding of words, phrases and simple writing; use a dictionary.
