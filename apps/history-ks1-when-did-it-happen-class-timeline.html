When Did It Happen? Class Timeline

Teacher aim: pupils sequence photographs from the past to today on a left-to-right time ribbon, building chronology and graded time vocabulary (long ago, a while ago, recently). Drag a photo into the right place; the app snaps it and reads the matching time phrase aloud.

Our time words

long ago: many, many years before now a while ago: when grown-ups were children recently: today, or just a short time ago before & after: which one came first a decade: ten years
