KS1 Music • Exploring the dimensions of music

High Low Slider 🎈

Teaching aim: children hear and see that when a sound goes up it gets higher, and when it goes down it gets lower. Then they explore loud, soft, fast and slow.

Move the balloon up and down

Drag the balloon high in the sky for a high sound. Pull it down near the grass for a low sound. You can also use the arrow keys.
