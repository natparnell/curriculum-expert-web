How to use this on the carpet

  • Show the tricky task. Ask: "Who is brave enough to have a go?" Effort counts, getting it perfect does not.
  • Tap Have a go! each time a child tries. A new leaf grows, even if the task was wobbly.
  • Talk about the kind, encouraging words that appear. The leaves show our effort adding up.
  • Use New tricky thing to pick another challenge. Use Start again for a fresh tree.
  • Keyboard and tablet friendly: every button works with touch, mouse, or the Tab and Enter keys.
