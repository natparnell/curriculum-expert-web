Phrase Remix 🇫🇷

Teacher aim: Pupils write phrases from memory and adapt them, swapping colour-coded words to describe people, places and things. They check that the adjective agrees with the noun, then add their sentence to the class gallery.

🎯 1. Choose a topic

Each topic gives you a model sentence to remix. Start here.

📝 2. Read the model sentence

This is the sentence you already know. You will keep the joining words and swap the coloured slots.

🔀 3. Remix your own sentence

Drag a word onto the matching coloured slot, or tap a slot then tap a word. On a keyboard: press a word to pick it up, then press a slot to drop it.
