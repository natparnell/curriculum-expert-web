Attainment 8 Bucket Builder

Teacher aim: Show how a pupil's Attainment 8 score is built from ten slots across four buckets, with English and maths each counting double. Drag the grades, fill the buckets, and watch A8 recalculate live.

Grade chips

Each chip is a GCSE grade (its number is the point score). Drag a chip into a bucket. Drag it back here to remove it.

Keyboard: Tab to a chip, press Enter or Space to pick it up, then Tab to a bucket and press Enter to drop it. Press Esc to cancel a pick-up.

Demonstration tool. All names and grades are fictional. Attainment 8 is the sum of ten slots: English (counts double, best of English Language or Literature when both are sat), maths (counts double), three EBacc qualifications, and three open qualifications. The score reported in performance tables is this points total divided by 10.
