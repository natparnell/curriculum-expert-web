How to use it on the carpet: project a painting, then invite children up to tap a face and a colour. After each tap, read out the prompt that appears and ask a child to answer in their own words. There are no wrong answers: the picture below the swatch shows that every feeling is welcome. Vocabulary to model: bright, dark, calm, busy, warm, cold, happy, sad, excited, gentle.
