🏝️ Survive the Island

Science · Key Stage 2 · Evolution and inheritance

Teacher aim: Pupils change an island's environment, then choose which inherited trait the birds carry. Over several generations they watch natural selection pick the best-adapted variation, and read the fossil clue that explains why.

1 Choose what changes on the island

An environment is the place where animals live, with all its conditions. Change it and you change which animals do well.

2 Pick the trait the birds inherit

Choose one feature. Every chick is born with it (this is inheritance). Will it help them survive the change you made?

3 The fossil clue

Scientists cannot watch the past. They study fossils, the preserved remains of living things from long ago, to work out how animals changed.

adaptation a feature that helps an animal survive where it lives variation small differences between animals of the same kind inheritance features passed from parents to their young natural selection the best-suited animals survive and have more young fossil preserved remains that give evidence of the past
