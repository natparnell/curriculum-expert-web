✨ Ask a Grown-Up ✨

Teacher aim: EYFS early online safety. Children meet pop-ups and unknown buttons on a pretend tablet, vote together on what to do, and learn the safe habit: stop and tell a trusted grown-up. Project this on the whiteboard and tap with the class.
