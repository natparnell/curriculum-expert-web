What does a strong SMART goal include?
A strong goal names a specific focus (a class, skill or behaviour), states a measurable indicator (a percentage, a count, an observable change), is realistic for one cycle, ties to a standard, and gives a deadline or review point. The meter rewards each of these.

End-of-cycle reflection

Pick a goal, then write a structured reflection. The journal scores the depth of your reflection and prompts for anything missing, so you arrive at appraisal with evidence, not just opinion.

Reflections that name impact, cite evidence and set a next step score highest.
