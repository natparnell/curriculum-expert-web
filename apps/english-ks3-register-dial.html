🎛️ Register Dial

Turn the dial and watch one message change its register to suit a new audience and purpose.

English Key Stage 3 Writing for purpose and audience
Teacher aim: Pupils see, concretely, how register (level of formality) shifts vocabulary, contractions, greetings, sign-offs, sentence structure and tone when the audience and purpose change. Links to the writing process: drafting the same idea in different forms (text, email, formal letter).

1Choose or write a message

Pick a worked example, or type your own short message in plain, everyday words. Then move to the dial below.

Tip: write it the way you would say it to a friend. The dial does the rest.
