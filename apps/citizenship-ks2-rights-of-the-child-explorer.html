Rights of the Child Explorer

Teacher aim: Pupils learn that every child has rights under the United Nations Convention on the Rights of the Child (UNCRC), match each right to a real classroom situation, then balance it against a matching responsibility we all share. Strand: rights, responsibilities and the rights of the child.
