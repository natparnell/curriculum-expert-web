Teacher notes and discussion prompts

Use this as a live, projector-friendly model. Run it whole-class, then let pupils experiment in pairs.

  • Inference, not just sharing: show that Jay never typed "I live in Riverside", yet the school post plus a location tag lets a stranger guess it. This is the key idea of a digital footprint.
  • Public vs friends: flip the visibility toggle and watch the verdict change. Discuss who "friends" really are online.
  • Misinformation and conduct: the "shared a rumour" post is flagged. Discuss sharing unchecked claims and respectful, legal online behaviour.
  • Tighten it up: challenge pupils to get the footprint below 30% while Jay still uses the account. Then press "Make Jay safe" to compare.
  • Talking point: a footprint can rarely be made zero, and old posts can stay visible for years.
