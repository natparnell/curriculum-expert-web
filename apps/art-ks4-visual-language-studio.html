For the teacher

  • Curriculum link (KS4 Art and Design): develops and refines control of the formal elements, deliberately using colour, tone, scale and texture to communicate intent, mood and meaning.
  • Suggested use: project the studio and isolate one element at a time. Ask pupils to predict the mood change before you move the slider, then read the live analysis to check their reasoning and vocabulary.
  • Assessment talk: “Justify two choices that make this image feel more ominous, naming the formal element each time.” The brief and match meter give pupils a self-marking target for analysis using subject terminology.
