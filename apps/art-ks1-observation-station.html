Observation Station 🔍

Look closely, then draw what you really see.

Teacher aim: Pupils observe a real object, trace its true outline as the photo fades, then build their drawing in a sketchbook frame. Covers KS1 Art and Design: using drawing to develop and share ideas, and recording from observation, imagination and experience.

Drawing station

Keyboard: click the drawing area, then use arrow keys to move the pencil. Hold the space bar while moving to draw a line. Press U to undo.
