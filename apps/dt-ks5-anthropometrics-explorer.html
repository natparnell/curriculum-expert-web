What is a percentile? (quick recall)

A percentile tells you what proportion of a population sits at or below a measurement. The 5th percentile woman is small: only 5% of women are shorter. The 95th percentile man is large: only 5% of men are taller.

Designers usually size for a range (often 5th to 95th, covering 90% of users) and use adjustability to reach the extremes. Some safety-critical dimensions use a single boundary: clearance is sized for the largest user (95th), reach is sized for the smallest user (5th).
