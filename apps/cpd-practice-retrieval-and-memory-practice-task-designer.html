Practice Task Designer

Teacher aim: design a set of practice items that moves from guided to independent, calibrating difficulty so each step is reachable and giving the minimal effective help: just enough support to keep pupils accurate, then fading it deliberately.
Step 1

Choose a learning objective to build practice for

Pick one. The item pool below changes to match it. Names and classes are fictional.

Step 2

Sequence the practice ladder

Drag each practice item into the band that matches its level of support. A strong sequence starts fully guided, fades to partial support, then reaches independent practice at the right difficulty. Tablet, whiteboard and keyboard all work.

Top band = most help. Bottom band = no scaffold.
