Pinch pot

Start at the base. Press your thumb into a ball of clay, then pinch and turn to open out the walls. Good for small, rounded pots.

Ready to begin. Pick a method, set your wall control, then press Add clay to the pot. Watch the form grow, then spin it to check every side.

✅ Potter's checklist

  • Use the pinch method
  • Use the coil method
  • Use the slab method
  • Build the pot at least 5 layers tall
  • Keep your walls even (good wall control)
  • Rotate the pot a full 360 degrees
