Decision Maths: Networks & Algorithms

Teaching aim: Build a weighted network or load a worked example, then step through the core Decision 1 algorithms one move at a time. Watch Dijkstra label vertices, Prim and Kruskal grow a minimum spanning tree, critical path analysis run its forward and backward passes, and a graphical linear programming solver shade a feasible region and slide the objective line to the optimal vertex.
