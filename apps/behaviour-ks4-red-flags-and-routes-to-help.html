Red Flags and Routes to Help

Spot the warning signs of unhealthy and abusive relationships, then map the safest route to support.

Teacher aim

Pupils recognise warning signs of abuse, coercive control and harassment in realistic scenarios, then identify safe, trusted routes to report and seek help.

Curriculum link (KS4 RSHE)

  • The characteristics of unhealthy and abusive relationships.
  • What coercive control, harassment and stalking look like.
  • That abuse can be physical, emotional, online or financial.
  • How and where to report concerns and access support.

Key vocabulary

Coercive control Harassment Consent Isolation Trusted adult Safeguarding

How to use

  1. Read each scenario and tap the lines that show a warning sign.
  2. Check your flags for instant feedback.
  3. Drag the support options into the safe or unsafe route.
  4. Move through all four scenarios.
