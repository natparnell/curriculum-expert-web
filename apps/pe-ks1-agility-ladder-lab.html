Agility Ladder Lab 👟

Teacher aim: project a footwork ladder, choose a drill, then speed it up. Arrows and glowing feet show pupils exactly where each foot lands so they build agility and coordination.

How to use it

  1. Pick a drill. Read the foot pattern out loud as a class.
  2. Tap Step once to walk through it slowly together.
  3. Tap Start drill and slide the speed up as pupils get smoother.
  4. Watch the feet: pink is the left foot, blue is the right foot.
