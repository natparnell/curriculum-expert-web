What do these measures mean?

Progress 8 (P8) compares each pupil's Attainment 8 score with the average for all pupils nationally who had the same KS2 prior attainment. Zero means in line with similar starters; positive means more progress; negative means less.

Attainment 8 (A8) is the raw points score across eight qualifications (out of roughly 90).

Basics (4+) is the percentage securing a grade 4 or above in both English and maths.
