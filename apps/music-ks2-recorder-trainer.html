🎵 Recorder Trainer

Learn the fingering, then play and let the app check your note is in tune.

Teacher aim: KS2 Music, performing. Pupils develop accuracy and control on the descant recorder by reading a fingering chart, playing the note, and using the live tuner for instant feedback on pitch. Suitable for whole-class instrumental teaching on a whiteboard or tablet.

How to play it

  • Open circle = leave it uncovered.
  • Half circle = cover only half.
