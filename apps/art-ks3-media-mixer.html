KS3 Art and Design · Media, materials and techniques

Media Mixer

Teacher aim: pupils draw one simple subject, then switch it between charcoal, ink wash, lino print and collage to investigate how each medium changes mark and mood. Builds proficiency across a range of 2D and 3D media.

1 Draw your subject

Pencil sketch
7 px
Turn on, then use arrow keys to move and Space to stamp a mark.

Tip: draw once, then try every medium. The same lines are re-interpreted each time.

2 Switch the medium

Make a mark, then choose a medium

Each medium handles the same drawing differently. Watch what happens to the line, the tone and the feeling.

3 Match the brief

Vocabulary you should be using: mark-making, tone, contrast, texture, line, mood, medium.
