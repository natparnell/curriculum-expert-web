Force Diagram Lab

Maths · KS5 Mechanics

Teacher aim: build a free-body diagram for a body on an inclined plane or a connected-particle pulley system, resolve forces parallel and perpendicular to the motion, then apply F = ma live to predict acceleration and tension. Drag forces on, adjust the conditions, and watch the resultant respond.
