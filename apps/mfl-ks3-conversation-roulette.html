🏫 How to run it

  • Warm up: learners draft a reply with the chips, read it aloud, then bin the chips and say it again from memory.
  • Real talk: the curveball pushes pupils to recover politely. Insist on a connective so the conversation develops, not just one-word answers.
  • Off the cuff: hide the chips, spin, and give five seconds of thinking time only. Spontaneity is the goal here.
  • Pair pupils: one spins and plays the partner, the other responds, then swap. The curveball is theirs to deliver with a straight face.
