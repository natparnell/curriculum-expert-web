🏃 Coach's Eye

Teacher aim: pupils compare a performance with a personal best, mark what went well and one thing to improve, then set a clear next step. Builds honest, kind peer assessment.

PE · KS2 Evaluating performance and improvement Works offline · tablet ready

1Choose a performance to coach

Each clip is a side-by-side diagram of a skill. The dashed gold line is that athlete's personal best. The green line is today's attempt.

2Mark up the clip

Pick a pen, then tap a spot on today's attempt (right) to drop a marker. Place at least one strength and exactly one improvement. Drag markers to fine tune.

Personal best target shape

Today's attempt mark this one

Strength marker Improvement marker - - - personal best line

Keyboard: the right diagram is focusable. Press Enter to drop a marker in the centre with the current pen, Tab to a marker then arrow keys to move it, Delete to remove it.

3Write your coaching feedback

Good coaches are specific and kind. Choose a sentence starter, then say exactly what you saw.
