Migration to Britain: The Long View

Teacher aim: pupils trace 2,000 years of arrivals to Britain on one map, then reason about continuity and change in who came and why. History, KS3: local history and pre-1066 consolidation.

Your enquiry: Britain has been a place people arrive at for thousands of years. Slide through time, tap each wave of arrivals on the map, and gather evidence. Then decide: across two millennia, what stayed the same about why people came, and what changed?
