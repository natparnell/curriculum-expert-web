🗣 Debate Builder

Teacher aim: pupils build a structured argument from claim, evidence and rebuttal cards, then deliver and challenge it courteously.

Spoken language: presentations, debate and reasoned justification (KS2)
1 Pick a motion
2 Build the argument map
3 Deliver and challenge

📜 Choose your motion

A motion is the statement the debate is about. Pick one, then choose whether you are arguing FOR it (you agree) or AGAINST it (you disagree).

🗺 Argument map

Place the right card in each slot to build a complete, persuasive argument.

1. Claim
Drop your main point here. This is what you want the class to believe.
2. Evidence
Drop a fact or reason that backs up your claim.
3. Rebuttal
Drop a polite reply to what the other side might say.

🎤 Your speech

Read this aloud to the class. Speak clearly, look up, and pause at the full stops.
