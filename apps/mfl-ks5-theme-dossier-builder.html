Theme Dossier Builder

A-level Modern Foreign Languages · Cultural, social and political themes of target-language societies

Teacher aim: pupils tag authentic articles and clips with arguments and statistics, sorting each into a theme dossier to build an essay-ready evidence bank.

📰 Source desk: Immigration

Each source carries draggable tags. Blue tags are arguments (a claim or idea); amber tags are statistics (a figure or proportion). Drag each tag, or select it and press Enter, into the matching column of the dossier on the right.

Keyboard: use Tab to reach a tag, press Enter or Space to file it into its column. The dossier tells you at once whether it sits in the right place.
