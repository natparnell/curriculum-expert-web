🔨 What Shall We Make?

Teacher aim: Children plan a design and technology project by saying what they will make, who it is for and what it needs to do, then choose materials and check their finished model against their plan. Covers EYFS designing and making for a purpose.

1What to make

Step 1: What shall we make?

Tap a picture to choose your idea. Each idea has a job to do.

Step 2: Who is it for?

Tap who will use the thing you make.

Step 3: Choose your materials

Drag materials into the planning board, or tap one then tap Add. Pick at least two. Some materials work better for your job than others.
