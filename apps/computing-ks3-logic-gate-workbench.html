Logic Gate Workbench 🔌

Teacher aim: pupils wire AND, OR and NOT gates between switches and a bulb, then watch the truth table and output update live as they flip inputs. Builds understanding of Boolean logic and logic circuits.

🧰 Add a gate

Tap a gate to drop it on the board. Then connect ports by tapping an output, then an input.

How to use the workbench
  • Two switches (A and B) and one bulb are fixed on the board.
  • Tap a switch to flip it between 0 and 1.
  • Add gates, then tap an output port (right side, hollow circle), then tap an input port (left side) to draw a wire.
  • Wire something into the bulb to light it.
  • The truth table is generated from your circuit automatically; the highlighted row is your current switch setting.
  • Keyboard: use the buttons and the switch buttons; every control is reachable by Tab.
