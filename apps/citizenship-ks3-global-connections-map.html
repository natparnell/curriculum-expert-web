Quick facts for your notes
  • United Nations (UN): almost every country in the world is a member. It works on peace, human rights, health and climate. The UK is one of five permanent members of the Security Council.
  • NATO: a defence alliance of European and North American countries. If one member is attacked, the others agree to help defend it.
  • Commonwealth: a voluntary group of 56 countries, most with historic ties to Britain. It is a partnership for trade, sport (the Commonwealth Games) and shared values, not a defence or law-making body.
