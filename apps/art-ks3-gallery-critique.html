Gallery Critique 🎨

Teacher aim: run a live KS3 gallery walk. Project an anonymous piece, then have the class tag genuine strengths and next steps against the assessment objectives, before pupils self-evaluate in the same language. National Curriculum strand: critically analysing and evaluating their own and others' creative work.
