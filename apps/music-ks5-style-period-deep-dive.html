Style Period Deep Dive

Music · KS5 / A level · History, style and contextual study

Teacher aim: students investigate the history, conventions and influential figures of each prescribed area of study, then test stylistic understanding through comparative listening and feature analysis with immediate, targeted feedback.

Choose an area of study

Select a prescribed style period to read its historical context, defining conventions and influential figures. Your choice also sets the focus for the listening and detective tasks.

Comparative listening: Baroque motif

Two short synthesised motifs are generated in the browser (no recordings, no internet). Excerpt A is written in the conventions of your chosen period. Excerpt B deliberately breaks one convention. Listen, then identify which feature is stylistically out of place. Targeted feedback explains why.

Excerpt B (one convention broken)

Which convention has Excerpt B broken?

Listen to both excerpts, then choose the feature in B that does not fit the style.

Tip: switch periods on the Context Explorer tab to regenerate the listening pair.
