💭 Wonder Wall KS1 RE

Teacher aim: children ask their own "big questions", sort them by the kind of wondering they are, and discover how different traditions and people respond. Strand: big questions and learning from religion (meaning, right and wrong).

1 Build an "I wonder" question

Tap a starter, then add your own ending. When it is ready, post it to the wall.

Your question will appear here.
Pick a starter to begin.

2 Sort the wonderings

Drag each sticky note (or use a keyboard: focus a note, press Enter to pick it up, then press 1, 2 or 3 to place it). Match the question to the kind of wondering it is.

✨ 1. Big meaning 0

❤️ 2. Right & wrong 0

🌍 3. People & beliefs 0

📝 Questions to sort

Drag a note into the column that matches it.

3 How do different people respond?

Tap a big question to see how people from different traditions might answer. There is no single right answer to a wondering: that is what makes it big.
