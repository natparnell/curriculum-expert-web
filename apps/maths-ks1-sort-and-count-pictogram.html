Sort and Count Pictogram

Teaching aim: sort objects into groups, then read a pictogram and tally chart to total and compare. Drag each picture into its column, watch the chart grow, then answer the questions the app builds from your own data. KS1 Statistics.

1 Sort the pictures

Drag each picture into the matching column. Or tap a picture, then tap a column. The pictogram and tally chart build themselves as you go.

Pictogram key: one picture stands for one object.

Keyboard: Tab to a picture, Enter or Space to pick it up, then Tab to a column and press Enter to drop it. Use the Send back button under a column to undo.

2 Tally chart

Each picture you sort adds one tally mark. Every fifth mark is a gate, so groups of five are easy to count.
