A-Level Essay Structure

Each paragraph should contain a Point (your argument), Evidence (specific historical detail), a Counter-argument (what could challenge your point), and an Evaluation (your judgement about which argument is stronger and why).

This PECE structure shows the examiner that you can construct and sustain a complex argument.
