Expectations and Norms Builder

Aim: help teachers articulate, communicate and track the visible behaviours that signal consistently high expectations and a positive classroom culture.

High expectations live in the words we use. Read each line a teacher might say, decide whether it is positively framed (names the wanted behaviour) or negatively framed (only names the unwanted behaviour), then rewrite the negative ones. The app gives you targeted feedback on each.
