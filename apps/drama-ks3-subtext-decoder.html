KS3 Drama • Performing from a script

Subtext Decoder

Teacher aim: pupils interpret a scripted line by choosing its hidden meaning (subtext) and the speaker's intention, then test how vocal and physical delivery changes what the audience actually hears. Covers text interpretation and rehearsing from a script.

1 Choose a moment

Each line is the same words, but the situation around it shapes what it could really mean.

2 Read the line in context

3 Decode the subtext

Drag one subtext (the real meaning under the words) and one intention (what the character wants to make happen) into the boxes. The app tells you how well each fits this moment.

Idea bank
💭 Real meaning (subtext)
Drop a subtext card here.
🎯 What they want (intention)
Drop an intention card here.
Keyboard: press Enter or Space on a card to pick it up, then press S to send it to the subtext box or I to send it to the intention box. Press a placed card's remove button to send it back.

4 Rehearse the delivery

Now stage it. Pick a tone, a pace, a pause and a physical action. The "audience reading" rebuilds live to show the meaning your choices create. Try to make the delivery match your chosen subtext.
