Physical Education · KS5 · Sport and society

Sport Through Time

Teacher aim: Pupils trace how sport developed from pre-industrial recreation to a global, commercialised, technology-driven industry, then test how social factors (class, gender, money, media, technology) opened or closed participation across each era.

1. Choose an era on the timeline

Move through five stages in the development of sport. Use the arrow keys or tap an era.

2. Toggle the social factors

Switch each factor on to apply the realities of the chosen era. Watch the modelled participation index respond, then read why.

Participation index 0 out of 100 (modelled)
Pick an era and toggle a factor to begin.

The participation index is a teaching model, not historical statistics. It shows the direction and scale of each factor's effect, not exact figures.

3. Apply it: source challenge

Read each scenario and choose the social factor that best explains the change in participation.
