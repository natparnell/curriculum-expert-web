Behaviour & Pastoral · KS2

Fuel Your Body

Teacher aim: Pupils explore how food, exercise, sleep and everyday choices about drugs, alcohol and tobacco affect how the body feels. Build a balanced plate, plan a week of activity, set bedtime and make sensible choices; watch your character Robin respond.

STEP 1 Build a balanced plate

Drag food onto the plate, or focus a food and press Enter. A balanced plate has plenty of vegetables and fruit, some starchy carbs, some protein, a little dairy and only a small treat.

STEP 2 Plan a week of activity

Tap each day to set it: rest, active, or lots of activity. Aim for about 60 minutes of activity most days.

STEP 3 Set the bedtime

Children aged 7 to 11 need about 9 to 11 hours of sleep each night. Slide to choose how many hours Robin sleeps.

STEP 4 Everyday choices

Some things we put in our bodies help us, some can harm us. Pick the choice you think keeps Robin healthy and safe.
