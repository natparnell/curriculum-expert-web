🧭 Expedition Planner OAA · KS3 PE

Teacher aim: pupils work as a team to plan a Dartmoor route under time, weather and equipment constraints, then test their leadership and problem solving as the app simulates the consequences of every choice.

📋Mission brief

Pick your conditions, then build a route from Base Camp to the Summit Refuge.

Weather changes which paths are safe and how fast you can move.

Kit chosen (drag on the budget of 3)

Your team can carry only 3 items. Choose wisely for the forecast.
