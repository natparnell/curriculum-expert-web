Volcano Cross-Section

Teacher aim: Pupils explore inside a volcano, watch the magma rise and erupt, then label each part of the cross-section to learn how a mountain that volcanoes build is shaped.

1. Explore inside the volcano

Tap, click or use Tab and Enter on a glowing part to find out what it does. Find all four, then press Erupt!

Find all four glowing parts to switch on the Erupt button.

Start exploring

Choose a glowing part of the volcano to begin.

2. Label the cross-section

Drag each word to the right box, or tap a word then tap a box. You can also use Tab and Enter with the keyboard.

Word help
Magma chamber
A big pool of hot, melted rock deep underground.
Main vent
The pipe that carries magma up to the top.
Crater
The bowl-shaped dip at the very top where magma comes out.
Lava
Magma is called lava once it reaches the surface.
Ash cloud
Tiny bits of rock and gas thrown high into the sky.
