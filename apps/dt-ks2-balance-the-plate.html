Balance the Plate

Teacher aim: Pupils apply the principles of a healthy and varied diet, plan and sequence a simple savoury dish using a range of techniques, and explain where food comes from and when it is in season. KS2 Design and Technology, Cooking and nutrition.

Build a balanced plate

Drag foods from the tray onto the plate, or select a food then press the plate. A balanced meal uses the five groups in the right amounts. Watch the meters and read the feedback, then adjust your plate.

Food tray

Tip: tap a food to pick it up, then tap the plate. Or drag it across.

Plate is empty. Add at least one food from each of the main groups to get started.

Group sizes follow the UK Eatwell Guide proportions: roughly a third fruit and vegetables, a third starchy carbohydrates, with smaller amounts of protein, dairy, and just a little oils and spreads.

Plan a savoury dish: vegetable pasta salad

Chef Aisha is making a cold vegetable pasta salad for a class picnic. The steps are jumbled. Put them in a safe, sensible order. Drag a step into the plan, use the up and down buttons, or use the keyboard. Each step names the technique it uses.

Steps to use (jumbled)

Drag into the plan on the right, or focus a step and press the up or down arrow keys.

    Check and feedback

    Read the steps, then decide which comes first. Think about hygiene, safety, and what has to happen before something else can.
