Composition Lab

Teacher aim: Pupils choreograph an eight-phrase sequence by combining motifs with the compositional devices of unison, canon and changes in dynamics, then preview the staged piece and refine it against a performance evaluation. Targets the KS3 strand: developing advanced composition in dance and gymnastics.
