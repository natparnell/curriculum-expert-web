Drama · Key Stage 2 · Performing scripts and poems aloud

Read-Aloud Rehearsal Room

Teacher aim: Pupils rehearse and perform a play script aloud with expression, watching the cues and pace markers so they speak clearly, in turn, and at the right speed.

1 Choose your part

Tap the character you are playing. Your lines will glow purple so you always know when it is your turn. Choose Whole class to read everyone together.

2 Rehearse the scene

Press Start cueing. The Rehearsal Room highlights each line in order and reads it aloud at the marked pace. When a glowing line is yours, take a breath and say it with feeling.

Ready when you are. Choose a part, then press Start cueing.
Scene: the school garden, early morning. Robin and Asha are planting sunflower seeds when Old Mr Pebble appears at the gate.
✋ pause take a short breath 🐌 slow say it gently ✨ bright sound excited (whispers) a stage direction

3 Perform it yourself

Turn the cueing off and read your own lines aloud. After your scene, tell the Rehearsal Room how you performed and get a coaching tip.
