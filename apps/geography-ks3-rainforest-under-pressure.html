🌳 Rainforest Under Pressure

Adjust the human pressures on a tropical rainforest and watch the ecosystem respond over 40 years.

Teacher aim: pupils investigate how deforestation from logging and farming, set against protected land, changes biodiversity, stored carbon and indigenous territory in a rainforest biome.

🎛️ Human pressures (per year)

Logging intensity 3

Selective and clear-fell logging. High logging clears canopy fast and fragments habitat.

Farming expansion 3

Cattle ranching and soy. Slash-and-burn clears land permanently and can trigger desertification.

Protected land 2

Reserves and indigenous territory. Shields canopy from clearance and lets cleared land regrow.
