🌳 Tidy Town

Teacher aim: Pupils learn they share a responsibility to look after the places and living things around them. They sort dropped litter into the correct recycling bin and watch the park's happiness grow. (Citizenship, KS1: caring for our world.)

How to play: pick up a piece of litter, then drop it in the bin where it belongs. Clean the whole park to fill the happiness meter.
