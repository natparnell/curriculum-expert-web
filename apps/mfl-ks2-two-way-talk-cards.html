Two-Way Talk Cards 🗣️

KS2 Modern Foreign Languages: hold a real conversation, then make it your own

Teacher aim: pupils ask and answer questions in a two-way exchange, then substitute their own details to rebuild the dialogue and present it orally.

Language Cards completed: 0 of 5

Step 1: tap a question card (your partner asks you)

Tap a question card above to see the conversation. 👆

Step 3: make it your own

Drag a card into the gap to rebuild the answer with your own detail. You can also use the keyboard: press a card, then press the gap. Then read your new answer aloud to your partner.

🎉 All five exchanges complete

Brilliant talking. Now pair up and perform two of the conversations to the class, swapping who asks and who answers.

Demonstration content for classroom modelling. Speaker names such as Amelie and Mateo are fictional. Tap the speaker buttons to hear native-language pronunciation where your device supports it.
