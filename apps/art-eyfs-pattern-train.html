🚂 Pattern Train

Art and Design · Early Years · Colour, shape and pattern

Teaching aim: children name colours and shapes, then continue a repeating pattern (AB and ABB) by adding the right carriages. The train only chuffs along when the pattern is correct.

Pick the carriage that keeps the pattern going.
For the teacher
  • EYFS Expressive Arts and Design and Mathematics (pattern): continue and copy repeating patterns; name primary colours; recognise simple shapes.
  • AB mode repeats two colours (for example red, blue, red, blue). ABB mode repeats a three-step unit (for example red, blue, blue).
  • Ask children to say the pattern aloud as they build: "red, blue, red, blue".
  • Carriages show both a colour and a shape word so colour-blind friendly cues are present.
