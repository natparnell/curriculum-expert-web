Critique the Product

KS3 Design and Technology · Evaluate, test and critique existing products

Teacher aim: Pupils dissect a real product, judge it against a written specification, weigh up ergonomics and sustainability, then propose evidence-based improvements. Covers the National Curriculum Evaluate strand: testing and critiquing against a specification, and investigating past and present design and technology.
