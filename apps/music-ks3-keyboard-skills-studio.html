How to use this in the lesson
  • Computer keys: the white-key row is ASDFGHJKL for the right-hand octave, and ZXCVB for the left-hand chord octave. Black keys sit on the row above.
  • Tablet or whiteboard: tap the keys directly. You can press more than one key at once for chords.
  • Control: aim to play melody notes exactly as they cross the gold line, not early or late. Watch your accuracy climb.
  • Expression: hold each chord smoothly under the melody so the two hands sound as one performance.
  • Progression: "Three-Note Builder" then "Folk Tune" then "Pop Riff". Turn off the highlight toggles once pupils can perform from notation alone.
