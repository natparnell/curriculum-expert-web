🏫 Build Our Class Rules

Teaching aim (KS1 Citizenship): understand why we have rules, why fairness and turn-taking matter, and how a class can decide together. Strand: rules, fairness and taking part.

1 Drag good rules onto the board

Drag each card from the tray onto our class board. Some cards are not kind or fair. Watch what the board tells you.

Rule cards

Keyboard: press a card with Tab, then press Enter or Space to send it to the board.

Our class board

Drop a rule card here to add it to our class rules.

2 Vote for our favourite rule

Everyone gets a say. Tap the plus button to give a rule a vote. The bars grow so we can see which rule wins. That is fair: we decide together.

Total votes so far: 0.

3 What happens with no rules?

Turn the rules switch ON or OFF and watch the playground. With rules, everyone takes a turn and shares. With no rules, it gets too busy and nobody is happy.
