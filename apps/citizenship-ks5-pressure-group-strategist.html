Pressure Group Strategist

Teacher aim: students run a single-issue pressure group over four phases, weighing insider tactics (lobbying, consultation, expert briefings) against outsider tactics (protest, media stunts, petitions). They observe how influence, media coverage, public support and policy change respond, then judge which mix actually shifts power.
