# PSHE, RSHE and Behaviour/Pastoral — curriculum coverage

*Source:* DfE statutory guidance "Relationships Education, RSE and Health Education" (2019, updated for Sept 2026) verified via gov.uk web search for primary Relationships Education and secondary RSE/Health Education statutory themes (families, caring friendships, respectful relationships, online and media, being safe, mental wellbeing, internet safety and harms, physical health and fitness, healthy eating, drugs/alcohol/tobacco, health and prevention, basic first aid, changing adolescent body). Non-statutory PSHE breadth (Living in the Wider World: economic wellbeing/careers, rights and responsibilities) drawn from PSHE Association programme of study and model knowledge. EYFS strands from the EYFS statutory framework (Personal, Social and Emotional Development). Behaviour and Pastoral strand set is WeST-defined (self-regulation, relationships and conflict, resilience and growth mindset, restorative practice, belonging and attendance, online/digital wellbeing).
