# Drama — curriculum coverage

*Source:* Drama is not a standalone National Curriculum subject in England: it sits within the EYFS framework (Expressive Arts and Design; Communication and Language), within English at KS1-KS3 (spoken language: role-play, improvisation, performing scripts, reading aloud, drama and performance), and at KS4/KS5 is defined by exam-board GCSE and A-level specifications. Strands assembled from: EYFS statutory framework; English NC 2014 spoken-language and reading programmes of study (KS1-3); and the GCSE/A-level Drama and Theatre exam specifications (AQA 8261, Pearson Edexcel 1DR0 GCSE and 9DR0 A-level, Eduqas), verified via web search May 2026. App list from /tmp/subject_apps.json key "Drama".

**Coverage: 66%** — 16 covered, 13 partial, 5 none, of 34 strands.
