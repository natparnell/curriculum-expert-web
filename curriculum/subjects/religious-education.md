# Religious Education — curriculum coverage

*Source:* Primary source: local knowledge files at knowledge/rs-curriculum/01_national_curriculum (eyfs.md, ks1.md, ks2.md, ks3.md, ks4.md, ks5.md, overview.md). RE is statutory but sits outside the National Curriculum: content is set locally by SACREs via locally agreed syllabuses, benchmarked against the REC National Content Standard 2023, with GCSE (DfE 2016 subject content) and A-level (DfE 2015 subject content) at KS4/KS5. Existing apps read from /tmp/subject_apps.json key "RE" (20 apps).

**Coverage: 54%** — 13 covered, 15 partial, 10 none, of 38 strands.
