*Source:* Primary source: local NC summary files at knowledge/science-curriculum/01_national_curriculum/ (eyfs.md, ks1.md, ks2.md, ks3.md, ks4.md, ks5.md). KS3/KS4/KS5 content sections supplemented with model knowledge of the statutory NC programmes of study (KS3) and standard GCSE/A-level subject content (Biology, Chemistry, Physics) where the local files focused on regulatory/pedagogical framing rather than listing every topic.
