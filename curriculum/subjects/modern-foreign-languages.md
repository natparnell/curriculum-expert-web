# Modern Foreign Languages — curriculum coverage

*Source:* Primary source: local NC summaries in knowledge/mfl-curriculum/01_national_curriculum (overview.md, eyfs.md, ks1.md, ks2.md, ks3.md, ks4.md, ks5.md). Strands framed around the four skills (listening, speaking, reading, writing), the Ofsted 2021 three pillars (phonics, vocabulary, grammar) and intercultural understanding, plus exam-board GCSE/A-level structures.
