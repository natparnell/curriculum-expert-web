# Music — curriculum coverage

*Source:* Statutory programmes of study confirmed via gov.uk (National curriculum in England: music programmes of study, 2013/2014) and the DfE non-statutory Model Music Curriculum KS1-3 (March 2021). EYFS framed against the EYFS statutory framework (Expressive Arts and Design: Being Imaginative and Expressive; Communication and Language). KS4/KS5 framed against typical GCSE and A-level Music exam-board subject content (AQA, Edexcel, Eduqas/OCR). Coverage cross-referenced against the WeST app library, which currently contains zero Music apps. Strand structure drawn from the inter-related dimensions of music plus the performing/composing/listening pillars.

**Coverage: 0%** — 0 covered, 0 partial, 34 none, of 34 strands.
