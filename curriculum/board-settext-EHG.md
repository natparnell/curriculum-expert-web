# Ivybridge Community College: Exam Boards and Set Texts (English, History, Geography)

Research compiled 10 June 2026 from Ivybridge Community College's published department pages and Sixth Form course descriptor PDFs at www.ivybridge.devon.sch.uk, corroborated by web search. Read-only research for WeST.

School: Ivybridge Community College (ICC), one of WeST's eight secondary schools.

Confidence key: "Confirmed" = stated explicitly in an ICC published source. "Unconfirmed" = not published by ICC; most likely value given with reasoning.
